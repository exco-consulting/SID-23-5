LM <<TMF_Vertical>>
The Business Value Development vertical grouping focuses on the definition, planning, design and implementation of value propositions and business promoting concepts/assets in the enterprise’s business portfolio. <br/>Frameworks artifacts in this vertical grouping create products, services, resources and business partner catalog information <br/>Business Value Development also creates sales and marketing campaigns and material.   <br/>Business Value Development understand the market across all key functional areas, the business environment, customer requirements and competition’s offerings in order to design and manage value propositions and business operations that succeed in their specific markets. <br/>The Business Value Development process the operations feedback and adjusts as required for the lifecycle management. <br/>
