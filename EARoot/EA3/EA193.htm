<<TMF_Vertical>>
The Strategy Management vertical grouping focuses on defining and managing business strategies and plans, gaining business buy-in and tracking the effectiveness of the strategies and adjusting as required.<br/>Frameworks artifacts in this vertical grouping focus on the analysis and definition of strategies and plans in support of all Development and Operations vertical groupings and is also responsible for establishing business commitment within the enterprise to support these strategies. This embraces all levels of operations from market, customer and products, through the services and the resources including strategies for involvement of business partners.<br/><br/>
