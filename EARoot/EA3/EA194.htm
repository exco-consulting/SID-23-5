CD <<TMF_Vertical>>
The Capability Management vertical grouping focuses on the development and deployment of capabilities including all infrastructures for the delivery of products and services as well as support infrastructures and all business enablers.<br/>Framework artifacts in this vertical grouping identify requirements, design and develop new or enhanced infrastructure and monitor the performance of the capabilities to adjust as required. <br/>
