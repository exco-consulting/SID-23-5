Partner strategy implementation application provides the capability to plan, implement and follow-up the progress of strategy adoption. It also provides the capability to streamline the partner strategy at organization level.<br/>Partner Strategy Implementation is the adoption of strategy by various levels of the organization. At different levels of organization, the strategy is translated into policies etc. for implementation and usage.<br/>
