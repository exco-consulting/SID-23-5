Resource Process Management (Workflow/Integration) involves the workflow planning and integration between resource provisioning and change processes. A vital component in this job is the logistics coordination. Delays and logistic disturbances can have a huge negative impact the resource projects and a jeopardy management with early discovery of disturbances and  that warns for consequences is vital.<br/>
