<<TMF_Application>>
Network Number Inventory Management provides the required functionality to manage dialable (telephone numbers) and non-dialable (network routing number) numbers. It can include both land-line and mobility numbers. It also handles ip type addresses (V4 or V6) along with any other network numbers of interest to a service provider. Numbers can be private or public.<br/>
