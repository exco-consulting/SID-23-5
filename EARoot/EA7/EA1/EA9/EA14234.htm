<<TMF_Application>>
Fallout Management applications manage the manual steps within an order flow. These steps might be due to an error, an exception, or simply a planned manual step. Fallout can come from order management applications within the customer, service, or resource layers After the fallout/manual steps addressed, the order flow will presumably continue from that point within the ordering flow.<br/>
