HSE (Healthy, Safety & Environmental) aspects of real estates, and equipment and services related to the real estates needs to be evaluated continuously to comply to applicable laws and relevant industry standards. To achieve a good HSE management there is a need for supporting applications to have knowledge about applicable laws and regulations, setting up policies and objectives with monitoring/follow up, and auditing and reviews.<br/>
