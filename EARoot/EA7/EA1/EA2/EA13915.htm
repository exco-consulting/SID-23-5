The Customer Bill Charge Calculation provides appropriate functionality to assemble items to be incorporated into a bill, applying prices or recalculating charges in the context of appropriate billing events. Customer Bill Charge Calculation also considers the results of aggregation and balance management functions in the process of calculating charges.<br/>
