<<TMF_Application>>
The Balance Management applications are responsible for activities related to the creation and maintenance of the balances of a customer and/or a subscriber. Balances may be shared (e.g. between subscribers in a hierarchy). Balance can be managed at most granule level for example at the level of subscription. These processes will be required to support both batch and real-time processing. Types of balances include:<br/>• Monetary balances <br/>   o Prepaid balances <br/>   o Postpaid balances <br/>•Non-monetary balances (e.g. free minutes, WAP-only quota, tokens, etc.)<br/>
