Business Intelligence (BI) refers to skills, technologies, applications and practices used to help a business acquire a better understanding of its commercial context. Business Intelligence may also refer to the collected information itself (from Wikipedia).<br/><br/>Business Intelligence/Data Warehousing (BIDW) is the delivery of business information required to effectively manage corporate operations. It includes both tactical and strategic decision making activities, compliance and regulatory reporting, and operational & transactional management/monitoring. <br/><br/>These functions are performed by a virtual team from various IT organizations within the enerprise.<br/>
