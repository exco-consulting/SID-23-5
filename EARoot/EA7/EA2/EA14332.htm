<<TMF_Application>>
The BI Performance Management is a set of technologies for measuring and planning the performance of business processes or physical resources such as network components or IT servers and services. Technologies for measurement may differ depending on resource type and are utilized lower in the architecture stack. Performance data is aggregated in the Analytics layer, but business rules are applied in this layer.<br/>
