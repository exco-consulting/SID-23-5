Patterns Domain Level 1 diagram
ClosedLoopWorkflow represents all the information needed to capture a workflow that realizes a specific objective in support of a ClosedLoop goal. <br/><br/>Two or more workflows constitute a ClosedLoop - the control or decision workflow(s), and the feedback workflow(s). Any these workflows that support the mandate of a ClosedLoop is identified as a ClosedLoopWorkflow. These ClosedLoop workflows are managed entities. Workflows in general may contain more than one activity necessary to realize an objective or an atomic goal.  <br/>
