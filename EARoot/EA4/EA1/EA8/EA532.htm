<<TMF_ABE>>
A generalized ABE and specializations that provide capacity and capacity demand for products, services, and resources. Capacity is the ability to provide some capability.  For example, one measure of network capacity is the ability of a network to provide a certain amount of bandwidth during the month of May. <br/>
