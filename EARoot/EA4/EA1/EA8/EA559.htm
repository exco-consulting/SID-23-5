<<TMF_ABE>>
The Policy Management ABE contains entities that are used to manage other entities in other Domains. This includes entities from other Common Business Entities sub-domains (e.g., Location and Party) as well as other sibling Domains (e.g., Product, Service and Resource).<br/>
