<<TMF_ABE>>
The Metric ABE defines standards of measurement including units and values and how the values are determined. It also includes thresholds used to evaluate the metric and the consequences of violating the thresholds. It’s in vision that other projects use and specialize the Metric ABE in some way (creating sub-classes and adding new entities) such as PM (Performance Management), RA (Revenue Assurance), SLA (Service Level Agreement), etc. <br/>
