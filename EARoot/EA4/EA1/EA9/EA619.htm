<<TMF_ABE>>
The Communication Interaction ABE contains all entities needed for specifying a CommunicationInteraction describing an exchange of information during a communication between two or more human(s) and/or machine(s) during a period of time. CommunicationInteraction might concern Parties playing a role of Customer, Supplier… or even Resource, therefore the ABE « CommunicationInteraction ABE » is located in « Common Business Entities Domain ». <br/>.\doc\Figure CI.04 - CommunicationIteractionItem Hierarchy.docx<br/>
