<<TMF_ABE>>
The Competitor ABE identifies other providers who compete in the same market segments, accumulates intelligence about the competitors, including products (price, Key Performance Indicators and so forth).<br/><br/>The Competitor ABE provides information relative to the analysis of intelligence pertaining to an enterprise’s competitors.  It is crucial to understand who the competitors are, what their products are, what the target markets for their products are, and how successful their products are.<br/><br/>The Competitor ABE and Competitor entities also comprise the following level-two ABEs<br/><br/>        • Competitor Intelligence<br/><br/>        • A variety of Product domain ABEs relative to Competitors<br/>
