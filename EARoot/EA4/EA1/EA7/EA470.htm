<<TMF_ABE>>
The Service ABE contains entities that are used to represent both customer-facing and resource-facing types of services. Entities in this ABE provide different views to examine, analyze, configure, monitor and repair Services of all types. Entities in this ABE are derived from Service Specification entities.<br/>
