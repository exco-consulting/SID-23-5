The Party Product Specification and Offering ABE represents the involvement parties playing roles have with ProductSpecifications and Product Offerings, such as ordered from and billed by. The sub-ABEs represent the intersection of entities in the two domains.  For example, PartyRoleProductOffering is the intersection between the ProductOffering and the PartyRole entities.         <br/>• These ABEs are only needed when a ProductSpecification and ProductOffering are on-boarded and offered to the market by an enterprise or when an offering is procured from another party.         <br/>• The ABEs should reside in the domain that contains the processes that manage their life cycles. The processes that manage the life cycle of the ABEs (create/update/delete the entities versus those that use the entities) are in the Business Partner domain, such as On-board and Off-board Product Offering from another party. .\doc\Figure PPSO.00 - Key Product Domain Business Entities.docx <br/>.\doc\Figure PPSO.02 - Party Role Product Offering.docx <br/>.\doc\Figure PPSO.03 - Party Role Product Offering and Product Specification.docx <br/>.\doc\Figure PPSO.04 - Party Role Prod Spec Subclasses.docx <br/>.\doc\Figure PPSO.06 - Fulfilled Sales Product Specification.docx<br/>
