[BPD-01] Business Partner ABEs Level 1 diagram
The diagram below lists the Business Partner Domain's ABEs.<br/><br/><b> Business Partner Party Roles ABE</b><br/>        • The Business Partner Party Roles ABE manages Partner roles and related information.<br/> <b>Business Partner Product Order ABE</b><br/>        • The Business Partner Product Order ABE contains all entities required to specify a communication used to procure, update or remove one or many Products in the context of a ProductOffering for the Service Provider.<br/><b>Business Partner Account ABE</b><br/>        • Business Partner Account ABE inherits from Account ABE and contains all entities needed for specifying basic attributes and relationships that describe an account. It also represents specificities in the context of a BusinessPartner.<br/><b> Party Product Specification and Offering ABE</b><br/>        • The Party Product Specification and Offering ABE represents the involvement parties playing roles have with ProductSpecifications and Product Offerings, such as ordered from and billed by.<br/><b> Party Revenue & Settlement ABE</b><br/>        • Party Revenue & Settlement ABE is composed of revenue sharing and settlements.<br/><b> Party Problem ABE (preliminary)</b><br/>        • Focuses on technical assistance and problem handling reported to and by other parties.<br/><b> Applied Party Billing Rate ABE (preliminary)</b><br/>        • <br/><b> Party Bill Collection ABE (preliminary)</b><br/>        • The Party Bill Collection ABE handles credit violations, actions for overdue debts (Dunning), and all what is related to BusinessPartnerPayment.<br/><b> Party Bill ABE (preliminary)</b><br/>       • <br/><b> Party Service Level Agreement ABE (preliminary)</b><br/>        • Party Service Level Agreement ABE represents an SLA with one or more other parties. See the Agreement ABE for additional details.<br/><b> Party Interaction ABE (preliminary)</b><br/>        • Party Interaction ABE represents communications with parties, and the translation of requests and inquiries into appropriate "events" such as the creation of an order, the creation of a bill inquiry, or the creation of a problem.<br/><b> Party Statistic ABE (not fully developed)</b><br/>        • Party Statistic ABE represents the analysis of party usage patterns, profitability statistics.<br/><b>Party Strategy & Plan ABE (not fully developed)</b><br/>        • The strategies and the planning of the business relation with the other parties with input from other ABEs, such as MarketSales, Party Performance and Competitor Analysis.<br/>
