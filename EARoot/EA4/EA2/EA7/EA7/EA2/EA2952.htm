A DunningRule is a type of PolicyRule.<br/><br/>It represents rules to apply to a dunning case: the events that trigger dunning rules evaluation, the conditions to evaluate and the actions that must be done (Ex: SMS, letters, outbound calls, barring/unbarring actions, late fees, penalties…)<br/>
