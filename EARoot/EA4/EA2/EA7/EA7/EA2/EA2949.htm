The DunningCase represents a type of cases open for a party. Whenever a party enters the dunning process, a dunning case is linked to his party account debt and it contains all details pertaining to the debt, such as dunning case rules, interactions… The status of a dunning case evolves according to internal or external events (payments made by the party, payment schedule granted…).<br/>
