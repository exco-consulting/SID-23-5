One or many PartyRoles might be involved in a BusinessPartnerProductOrder / BusinessPartnerProductOrderItem such as Distributor, Holder, Buyer or Product User.<br/>Each BusinessPartnerProductOrderItem requires an action (AllowedProductAction) on a ProductSpecification or a ProductOffering.<br/>The configuration required is described by the related Product even if it isn’t delivered yet. The prices applicable are related to the Product (ProductPrice).<br/>To deliver the corresponding Product, the Place to deliver and Appointment(s) might be needed. An Appointment is an arrangement to do something or meet someone at a particular time and location.<br/>A BusinessPartnerProductOrderItem may also specify the commitment chosen by the Service Provider (i.e. CommitmentTermOrCondition).<br/>The BusinessPartnerProductOrderItems may specify the BusinessPartnerAccount to which the Products’ are charged to.<br/>When BusinessPartnerProductOrderItems are not charged to a BusinessPartnerAccount and carry one-time charges, they might be directly paid through one or many BusinessPartnerPayments.<br/>
