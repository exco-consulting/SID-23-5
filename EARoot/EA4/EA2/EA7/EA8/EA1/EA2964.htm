PartySettlement items are defined in AppliedPartyBillingRate. For example, if a dealer’s settlement is $10 in total. The item details of the settlement are in AppliedPartyBillingRate such as $5 for a sale order, $3 for an upsell order, and $2 for a customer support. So, the corresponding customer charge details are in AppliedCustomerBillingRate.<br/>PartySettlement is created for a PartyAccount.<br/>The following figure shows the PartySettlement AppliedBillingRate model.<br/>
