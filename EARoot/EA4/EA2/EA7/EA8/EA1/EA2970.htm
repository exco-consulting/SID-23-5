Party Settlement is a group of entities that represent a monetary value that will be paid to a party by the enterprise or a party will pay to the enterprise as a result of the usage/recurring fee of a product/service/resource provided. Party Settlement is managed by agreements and can be created between an enterprise and other MVNOs, dealers, retailers, content providers, etc.<br/>
