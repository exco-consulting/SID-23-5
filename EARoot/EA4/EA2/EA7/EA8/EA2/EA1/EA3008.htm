Options diagram
<font color="#29313b">Two options might exist for an agreement between the Enterprise and the Party</font><br/><ul>
<li><font color="#29313b">either the Party 1 shares the revenue with the Enterprise. In this case, Party 1 directly shares revenue with the Enterprise and they have a <b>Revenue Sharing Agreement</b>.</font></li></ul>
2 sub-cases:<br/><ul>
<li><font color="#29313b">according to degree of trust, they accept the payment from Party 1 without controlling</font></li><li><font color="#29313b">or the Enterprise calculates to check what Party 1 owes producing a revenue sharing notice</font></li></ul>
<font color="#29313b">or the Enterprise may charge some type of fees (listing or marketing) for selling on behalf of Party 1. In this case Party 1 receives an invoice from Enterprise and no Revenue Sharing Agreement is required (not illustrated here)</font><br/>
