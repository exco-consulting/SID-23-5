<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
An adjustment to the revenue to be shared among an enterprise and one or more other parties. This could be required, but not limited to, based on changes to a revenue sharing model associated with a party revenue sharing agreement or based on discrepancies found by the Party Revenue Share Reconciliation process.<br/>
