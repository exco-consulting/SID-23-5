<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
An item that is used to determine revenue shared with another party for a ProductOffering. The item may be a ProductPrice paid to acquire an offering if a bill/invoice charge is not created for it or an AppliedParty/CustomerBillingRate, which could be a charge, discount, usage charge, and so forth.<br/>
