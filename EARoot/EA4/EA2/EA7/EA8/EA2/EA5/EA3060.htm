Notes: The specification of whether revenue is retained by an enterprise and whether a party participates in revenue sharing. For example, an enterprise may charge a listing fee for a party's offering or offerings as part of an agreement between a party and an enterprise, and an enterprise passes all revenue on to the party for sales of the party's offering. The party is invoiced separately for the listing fee. For a template revenue sharing model only an enterprise's involvement is specified. When a party enters into a revenue sharing agreement and a template is used their involvement is specified.<br/>
