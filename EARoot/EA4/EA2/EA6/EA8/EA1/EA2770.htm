A SalesOpportunity is an opportunity to generate revenue from a SalesLead that can already be an existing Customer. Opportunities are the pending deals that need to be tracked. Opportunities build the pipeline, which will contribute to the forecast. A SalesOpportunity represents an interest expressed by a Party known as a SalesLead to at least one ProductOffering/ProductSpecification proposed by the CSP.<br/>
