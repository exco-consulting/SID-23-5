A MarketingManager is a role played by a Party. A MarketingManager is responsible for the Marketing management process. It is the process  of developing strategies and planning for product or services, advertising, promotions, sales to reach desired customer segment. <br/><br/>The marketing campaign manager manages the definition, the planning, the budget, the execution and the monitoring of OperationalMarketingPlan and MarketingCampaigns. He is also in charge to validate the design and the implementation of the marketing campaign.<br/><br/>
