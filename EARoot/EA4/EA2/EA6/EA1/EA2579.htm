A Prospect is a type of PartyRole played by a Party specifying the Party is a potential buyer of Products from the Service Provider. A Party playing a SalesLead role may play later several other PartyRoles such as Prospect and eventually Customer. The Party playing a SalesLead might be a Customer for some offers or not. Businesses gain access to sales leads through advertising, trade shows, direct mailings, third parties, and other marketing efforts.<br/>
