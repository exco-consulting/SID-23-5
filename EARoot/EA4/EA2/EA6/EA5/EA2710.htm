A WaveList corresponds to the split of a MarketingCampaign’s target in waves. The reason of the split might be:<br/><br/>        • temporal splitting: wave recurrence, (ex: a non-recurrent Wave has only one Wave’s Recurrence);<br/><br/>        • technical splitting (ex: limited volume in email sending, SMS sending…).<br/>
