Figure SC.01-I01 Sales Commission overview – SalesCommisionSpecification for Retail Sales Agent diagram
John (PartyRole: employee) is an employee of the CSP and he is a RetailSalesAgent.<br/>The CSP provides Sales Commission to the retail sales agents, when they achieve a Sales target. <br/>Three commissions (SalesCommissionSpecification) are specified and triggered every first of a month (defined as SalesCommissionRule):<br/><ul>
<li>for handset device sold 5% of total amount if total amount is above $100</li><li>for accessory sold 6% of total amount if total amount is above $50</li><li>for Usage Volume Products (such as $50 national calls) sold $1 for each Usage Volume Product equal or above $50</li></ul><p/>
