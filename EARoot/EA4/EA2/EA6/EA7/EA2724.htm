Figure SC.03 Sales Commission applied diagram
The Figure “SC.03 – Sales Commission Applied” presents the SalesCommission main entities.<br/>The primary business entities in the Sales Commission model are SalesCommission, SalesCommissionSpecification, CustomerProductOrder, Product and ProductUsage. SalesCommission is applied to the PartyRole (Sales Agent, Partner, Employee etc.,) and is applied as per the ProductUsage, CustomerProductOrderItem, or CustomerBill. Additional business entities, unique to a given enterprise, can be added to extend this model.<br/>
