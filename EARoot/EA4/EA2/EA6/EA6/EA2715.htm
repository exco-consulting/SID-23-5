A MarketingIndicatorDefinition specifies a metric measure. It specifies measure (how to calculate it) with targeted values and associated actions when the thresholds aren’t achieved. <br/><br/>The MarketingIndicatorDefinitions are used to define objectives of OperationalMarketingPlans and MarketingCampaigns such as performance or budget objectives.<br/><br/>MarketingIndicatorDefinition is a type of MetricDefMeasure.<br/>
