MarketingObjectiveDefinition is a set of actions in charge of to specify, CSP communications and presence according to strategic axes, to support product introductions and specific promotions to sell products, retain customers, and bring in new customers.<br/><br/>Example:<br/><br/>        • drive profitable sales with marketing objectives such as sell on value, promote strategic products, improve sales cycle…<br/><br/>        • grow customer profitability with marketing objectives such as retain & grow strategic accounts, customer P&L sales focus…<br/><br/>MarketingObjectiveDefinition is defined by metrics to evaluate with threshold that must be achieved and possibly actions that must be applied when the thresholds are not achieved. These metrics and thresholds are specified with MktIndicatorDefinition.<br/><br/>MarketingObjectiveDefinition is a type of MetricDefinition.<br/><br/>Each MarketingObjectiveDefinition might take part in one or many MarketingObjectiveDefinitions.<br/>
