A characteristic quality or distinctive feature of a CompetitorProductCorrelation. The characteristic can be take on a discrete value, such as geographic disbursement (central, national, cascading), can take on a range of values, (for example, Competitor ProductOffering revenue of $500,000 - $1,000,000), or can be derived from a formula (for example, number of MarketSegments in correlation = number of MarketSegments related to this correlation).<br/>
