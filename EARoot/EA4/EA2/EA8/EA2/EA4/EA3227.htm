<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
Represents an implementation of a request for reservation of work time in some human resource pool. It is also possible to reserve the work time of an individual employee. Note: the reservation, or booking according to appointment, is associated to Calendar Entry. The CalendarEntry here can belong to a reservation WorkSchedule. This Calendar entry may be floating. Once the Reservation becomes an Assignment (to be dispatched), a particular time slot (Calendar Entry) can be fixed in the WorkSchedule.<br/>
