<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
Represents an assignment of a particular WorforceEmployeeRole for a particular work task in a specific timeframe. For example, assignment of a person in a network engineer role to a trouble shooting work task for at least 2 hours. The assignment is associated with a CalendarEntry of an Assignment WorkSchedule.<br/>
