PayRange defines a pay range for Employees. It specifies minimum, average and maximum amounts in the range.<br/>It provides information which is primarily used for the calculation of Compensation Ratio and Range Penetration. <br/>Usually Pay Range is linked to Pay Grade. <br/>By default Pay Range is defined by Geographical Zone, Pay Grade and Legal Entity.  e.g. Organisation may define the PayRange associated with specific PayGrade.  <br/>PayGrade G-1 - Sequence-1 can have Max Pay 70KGBP  Avg Pay 60K GBP and Min Pay 50K GBP<br/>
