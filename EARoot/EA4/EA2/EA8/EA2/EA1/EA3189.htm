A pay grade is a step that defines the amount of pay an employee will receive. The pay grade may or may not be defined by the level of the responsibilities performed within the job description of the position the authority exercised by the position and the length of time the employee has performed the job. This is generally associated with the Pay Range providing further details of the grades  steps and validity. <br/>
