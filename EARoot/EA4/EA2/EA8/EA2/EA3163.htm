Figure WFM.14 - Employee Pay diagram
PaySpecification specifies PayComponents that are part of a Pay in a PayRange. <br/>PayRange defines a pay range for Employees. It specifies minimum, average and maximum amounts in the range.<br/>Each PaySpecification is grouped in a PayGroup specifying the frequency of Payroll and related to the PayRollAgency in charge of executing payrolls.<br/>A PaySpecification is composed of PayComponentSpecification.<br/>PayComponentSpecification specifies components of a pay that might correspond to fixed salary, bonus...<br/>When a PayComponentSpecification corresponds to a rate (e.g. 10%), it specifies on which other PayComponentSpecification it applies on.<br/>In a company, each employee (WorforceEmployeeRole) benefits from an EmployeePay.<br/>The EmployeePay is composed of EmployeePayComponent.<br/>The EmployeePayComponent specifies the validity period of the component and the amount (unit -any currency- and the value such as $10) for the employee.<br/>
