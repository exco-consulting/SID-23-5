Figure WFM.14-I03 - PaySpecification and its components diagram
PaySpecification for Pay Range 123 and PaySpecification for Pay Range 234 have one component in common, the PC-Mobile-1.<br/>PC-Mobile-1 specifies an amount of 100$ per year that is paid monthly.<br/><br/>PaySpecification for Pay Range 123 has two other components:<br/><ul>
<li>PC-Basic-1 that represents a basic pay for employees from PaySpecification of Range 123. This component is defined yearly and paid monthly to the employee. There is no amount as it is chosen for each Employee respecting the minimum and maximum total amount defined by the PayRange.</li><li>PC-Variable-1 that represents the Variable pay for employees from PaySpecification of Range 123. This component is defined yearly and paid yearly. It represents 10% of the Basic Pay.</li></ul>
<br/>PaySpecification for Pay Range 23' has two other components:<br/><ul>
<li>PC-Basic-2 that represents a basic pay for employees from PaySpecification of Range 234. This component is defined yearly and paid monthly to the employee. There is no amount as it is chosen for each Employee respecting the minimum and maximum total amount defined by the PayRange.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>PC-Variable-1 that represents the Variable pay for employees from PaySpecification of Range 234. This component is defined yearly and paid yearly. It represents 15% of the Basic Pay.</li></ul><p/>
