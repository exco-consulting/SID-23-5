Figure RA.09 - RA Violation diagram
The Revenue Assurance Violation ABE models’ discrepancies identified based on applying the rules defined by Revenue Assurance Control entities as described in the section on Revenue Assurance Controls.<br/>Figure RA.09 depicts how a RevenueAssuranceParameterValue that does not meet a RevenueAssuranceObjective results in a RevenueAssuranceConsequence.  The consequence influences the selection of a RevenueAssuranceViolationSpec that results in a RevenueAssuranceViolation, which is also associated to the parameter value that triggered its creation.<br/>In the customer billing control, the value of true (1) means that the objective has not been met and results in a consequence named “Customer Service Not Billed”.  The consequence is related to a RevenueAssuranceViolationSpec named “Discrepancy in Customer Services Billed”.  The consequence describes the RevenueAssuranceViolation named “Customer X Billing Violation”, which is also related to the control value of true.<br/><br/>
