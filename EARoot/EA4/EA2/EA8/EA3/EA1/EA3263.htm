Figure RA.18 - RA Assessment Result diagram
The outcome of an assessment is defined by the RevenueAssuranceAssessmentResult business entity as shown in Figure RA.18.  A result may be a RevenueAssuranceRecommendation or may be an identified RevenueAssuranceTrend.  A trend is defined by its associated RevenueAssuranceParmValues and a RevenueAssuranceParameter as shown in the figure.  For example, a recommendation may be to expand the time period during with a KPI is applicable; a trend may show an increasing number of customers not billed over a month-long period.<br/>
