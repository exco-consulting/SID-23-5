<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A change of state that has significance for the security of a Configuration Item or IT Service.  SecurityEvents are designed to carry information about network defense-related activities that are sensed on networks and network-connected devices.  SecurityEvents can be either assessed in depth to determine if successful intrusions or compromises resulted, or they can be aggregated to characterize the threat environment.<br/>
