Figure E.00 - Enterprise Party Roles diagram
The Enterprise Domain party roles presently identified are:<br/><ul>
<li><b>ServiceProvider</b>: An organization or individual which offers products to others in exchange for payment in some form.</li><li><b>ComplementaryProvider</b>: A type of ServiceProvider that complements another provider.</li><li><b>ThirdPartyProvider</b>: A ServiceProvider besides the two primarily involved in a situation.</li><li><b>ServiceProviderEmployee</b>: A Service ProviderEmployee is an Individual who is employed by the Service Provider for wages or salary. This is modeled as an Individual playing the role of ServiceProviderEmployee . A ServiceProviderEmployee is a type of PartyRole.</li><li><b>Regulator</b>: A party that supervises a particular industry or business activity</li></ul><p/>
