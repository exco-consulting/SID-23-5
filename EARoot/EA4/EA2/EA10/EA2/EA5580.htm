Figure Perm.01.I02 – PermissionSpec and Permission for Customer PartyRole diagram
John Smith owns “My Broadband Contract” and therefore he plays the Role of Customer on it.<br/>The PartyRole Customer played by John Smith corresponds to the PartyRoleSpecification “Customer”, “My Broadband Contract” corresponds to the RootEntityType “ProductBundle/Contract” and “JS BillingAccount” to the RootEntityType “CustomerBillingAccount”.<br/>According to the Permission specification, implicit permissions on “My Broadband Contract” and “John Smith BA” will be allocated to the role Customer played by John Smith.<br/>
