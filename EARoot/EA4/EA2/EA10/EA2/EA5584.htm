We also may need to specify a scope for the permissions.<br/>PermissionScopeSpec defines through PermissionScopeSpecPolicyRuleSpec criteria that might be used to define the scope such as market segment, geographic area, time…<br/>To describe these criteria that might be complex a PermissionScopeSpec might be managed by one or many PolicyRuleSpec (PermissionScopeSpecPolicyRuleSpec).<br/>PermissionScope specifies for each criteria the value(s) for the Permission if different values are possible in the PermissionScopeSpec.<br/>To describe these criteria that might be complex a PermissionScopeSpec might be managed by one or many PolicyRuleSpec (PermissionScopeSpecPolicyRuleSpec).<br/>The PermissionScopePolicyRule inherits from PolicyRule and PermissionScopeSpecPolicyRuleSpec inherits from PolicyRuleSpec.<br/>
