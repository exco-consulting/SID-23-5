The Business Partner Domain roles presently identified are:<br/><ul>
<li><b>Partner</b>: A PartyRole, played by a Party being an equitable partner in a business interaction with other parties, playing the Partner role. Usually all Partner have the same (or even compatible) business objectives.</li><li><b>Supplier</b>: A PartyRole played by a Party, who commits to provide a deliverable to a Buyer by a formal Agreement (Commitment)</li><li><b>Carrier</b>: A Carrier is a type of Supplier played by a Company in charge of shipping material goods.</li></ul><p/>
