<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
The certificate describing an exemption from tax (partial or complete) granted for a party and/or a place. <br/><br/>Examples include:<br/><br/>    - Party-based<br/><br/> - Individual might be exempt due to health or other personal situation<br/><br/> - Organization might be exempt due to its business (e.g. a charity, or government)<br/><br/>    - Location-based<br/><br/> - A City might have a global exemption from purchase tax<br/>
