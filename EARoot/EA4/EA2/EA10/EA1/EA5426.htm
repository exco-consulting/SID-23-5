A PartyProfileSpec is a type of AtomicSegmentSpecification to specify rules for classifying Parties or PartyRoles.<br/>A PartyProfile is a type of AtomicSegment that classifies Party’s or PartyRole’s instances for which the CriterionCondition is “True” when it is built.<br/>A PartyProfile is the result of applying a PartyProfileSpec when a triggering Event occurs.<br/>A DemographicSegment is a type of PartyProfile where corresponding rules are based on feature or quality of the Party such as age, income, education, revenue, and so forth.<br/>A MarketSegment is a type of PartyProfile that groups Parties. MarketSegments are the target of MarketingCampaigns, ProductOfferings, product promotions, product placements, and product programs from both internal and external (Competitors, and other Suppliers) perspective.<br/>Please refer to the Common guide book section Segmentation ABE for additional information.<br/><br/>
