Figure Ppr.01 - Reminder about RootEntityType and RootEntity diagram
The Party Privacy Management process manages privacy information and agreement with party.<br/>Therefore, it needs to specify the information concerned by Privacy rules and the Privacy rules themselves.<br/><br/>The following Figure depicts how all information of the SID model might be addressed.<br/>Each SID Entity corresponds to an instance of RootEntityType and each attribute of an Entity corresponds to the use (RootEntityTypeCharUse) of a CharacteristicSpecification.<br/>Each possible value of a CharacteristicSpecification (CharacteristicSpecValue) might be used or not in the context of a RootEntityType (RootEntityTypeCharValueUse).<br/>Each instance of a SID Entity corresponds to an instance of RootEntity and the value of its attributes are carried by an instance of CharacteristicValue.<br/>
