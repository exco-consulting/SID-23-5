Figure Cal.07 - Organizer & Attendee diagram
Calendar Entries have an Organizer. The Organizer may have delegated to someone else and hence we keep track of both the Sender and the Organizer.<br/>When organizing meetings, requests are sent to a number of potential attendees. An attendee may be requested to participate or may be told their participation is optional.  Each attendee may have read or not read the request, which is represented by the requestStatus. Each attendee may then accept or reject the request to define their actual participation. <br/>In the same way that an organizer can delegate organizing a meeting, an attendee may delegate attendance.<br/>In the SID Party document, we defined that a PartyRole could be contacted in many ways via various contact media. <br/>The following Figure shows how this relates to a meeting attendee. <br/>
