<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
ServiceLevelSpecExpression describes strict relations between ServiceLevelSpecification subordinates (ServiceLevelSpecParameter, ServiceLevelObjective, ServiceLevelConsequence, and ServiceLevelApplicability)). The baseline idea of ServiceLevelSpecExpression is to resolve the multiple associations between its subordinates. For example, an expression may consist of a consequence and its applicability for a ServiceLevelSpecification.<br/>
