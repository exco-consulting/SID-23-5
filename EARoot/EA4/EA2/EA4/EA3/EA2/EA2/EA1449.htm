A ServiceBundleSpec is the base class for defining the different classes of bundled ResourceFacingServiceSpecs.<br/><br/><br/><br/>Conceptually, a ServiceBundleSpec is thought of as a collection to enable the needs of different sets of ResourceFacingServiceSpecs to be grouped together. The "bundle" conveys the concept of grouped ServiceSpecs that are related. Since these are ResoureFacingSpecifications, they define reusable templates for implementing the ResourceFacingServices that are required by a particular CustomerFacingService (as represented by a ServicePackage).<br/><br/><br/><br/>Therefore, a ServiceBundleSpec templatizes the shared characteristics of this service bundling.<br/>
