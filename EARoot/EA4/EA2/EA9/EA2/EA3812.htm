A ManagedEntity is used to specify that an Entity is manageable by electronic means.<br/><br/>It may produce and/or consume other ManagedEntities, and has a distinct state. A ManagedEntity may concern a simple concept, such as a logical point of communication, or a complex concept, such as an Application or System.<br/>
