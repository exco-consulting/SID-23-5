Figure Proj.00 - project vision diagram
Projects are executed to generate business value for the enterprise. This business value is specified by the Vision which addresses distinct BusinessObjectives of the enterprise. <br/>The Vision is the common understanding of all parties involved in a project (either directly or as a stakeholder) about the goals of a project, the key success criteria, timeline and impact of not doing the project.<br/>
