Figure Me.05 -I01 – MetricDefinitionInvolvement diagram
<font color="#29313b">An example of an involvement relating a metric (or rather its MetricDefinition) to a Party or PartyRole is given in following Figure.</font><br/><font color="#29313b">The involvement here refers to the responsibility of performing the measurement of the M1-Avg metric and reporting the results, presumably via a measurement system with a GUI for network and service administrators.</font><br/><font color="#29313b">This data model example would represent the point of view of a network operator which would contract a third-party company as a supplier of this measurement and reporting service. </font><br/><font color="#29313b">Note that the validFor attributes are different in each instance, since they describe the validity period of each object: validity of the metric involvement, validity of the entity modeled as a Party, and validity of the entity PartyRole as a supplier (which may or may not be the same as the validity of the metric involvement).</font><br/>
