<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A MetricManagementJob is a management activity that may consist of several steps but is performed as a single logical unit in a Metric context.<br/><br/>It can be a one-time activity or a regular one. In the latter case, a schedule definition is attached to it.<br/><br/>A MetricMeasurementJob defines a measurement job for a scope of monitored objects using the MonitoredMetricObjectCriterion.<br/>
