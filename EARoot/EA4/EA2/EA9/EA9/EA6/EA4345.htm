A MetricMeasurementObservation represents the contextual information of a measurement such as the observation period, the MetricMeasurementJob used,  the requester of the observation and the scope of the observation through the related MonitoredMetricObjectCriterion.<br/><br/>The MonitoredMetricObjectCriterion related to MetricMeasurementObservation has to be equal to or a sub-set of the one related to the concerned MetricMeasurementJob.<br/><br/>A MetricMeasurementObservation also specifies the MectricMeasures resulting from this observation.<br/>
