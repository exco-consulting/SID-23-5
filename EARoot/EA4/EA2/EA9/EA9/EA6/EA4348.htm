A MonitoredMetricObjectCriterion defines the scope of monitored objects for which a MeasurementJob is executed.<br/><br/>The scope is either defined:<br/><br/>• by a filter on a Class (sub-class MonitoredMetricClassCriterion) <br/><br/>• or by a list of instances (sub-class MonitoredMetricInstanceCriterion).<br/>
