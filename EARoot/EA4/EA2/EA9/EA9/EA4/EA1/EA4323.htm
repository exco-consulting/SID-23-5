A MetricDefMeasureThresholdRule is a rule that defines the condition (raise or clear) to achieve to apply consequences when a threshold (MetricDefMeasureThreshold) is crossed or ceased to be crossed. It also defines the severity of the raise or clear of the threshold.<br/>
