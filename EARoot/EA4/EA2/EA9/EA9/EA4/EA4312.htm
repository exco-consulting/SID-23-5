<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
MetricDefMeasureExpression describes strict relations between MetricDefMeasure subordinates (MetricDefMeasureThresholdRule, MetricDefMeasureApplicability, MetricDefMeasureConsequence). The baseline idea of MetricDefMeasureExpression is to resolve the multiple associations between MetricDefMeasure subordinates. For example, an expression may consist of a consequence and its applicability for a MetricDefMeasure.<br/>
