A MetricDefMeasureConsequence defines the action (prescribed action or notification) to take when a MetricDefMeasureThresholdRule is crossed.<br/><br/>The same MetricDefMeasureConsequence might be reused by several MetricDefMeasureThresholdRules.<br/><br/>Different consequences (MetricDefMeasureConsequence) can be defined for different severities.<br/>
