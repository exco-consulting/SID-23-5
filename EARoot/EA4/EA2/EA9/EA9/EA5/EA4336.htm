<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A numerical value or text determined for a MetricDefMeasure that may be collected for a RootEntity, such as a connection or a dealer.<br/><br/>A MetricMeasure might be part of a group of MetricMeasures realized at a specific moment (MetricMeasureGroup).<br/><br/>For example, a value of .005 ms that represents average packet delay.<br/>
