Figure Me.13 - Metric Threshold Job and MetricDefMeasureThresholdSet diagram
The following Figure shows the different types of MetricMeasurementJob.<br/>A MetricMeasurementJob may be<br/>        • either a job for evaluating thresholds defined over MetricDefMeasure (MetricMeasurementThresholdJob),<br/>        • a job for producing MetricMesures according to a MetricDefMeasure (MetricMeasurementProductionJob),<br/>        • a job for collecting MetricMeasures produced by another job (MetricMeasurementCollectionJob),<br/>        • or a job for collecting MetricMeasures produced by another job to make available MetricMeasures for subscribers. (AdhocMetricCollection).<br/>A MetricMeasurementThresholdJob evaluates one MetricDefMeasureThresholdSet.<br/>A MetricMeasurementJob defines a scope of monitored objects using the MonitoredMetricObjectCriteria.<br/>A MetricMeasurementJob also defines a scope of monitored objects using the MonitoredMetricClassCriteria.<br/>
