Figure G.03 - Goal Lifecycle diagram
This is an illustration to manage the lifecycle of "Goal" as a managed entity. It includes the definition and acceptance of a goal, having a goal that is inactive because of a lack of initialization with a time factor or allocation to a goal-owner, the ability to state the goal as in force and be able to suspend the goal when the ambition or aspiration needs holding off due to changes in expectation.   <br/>
