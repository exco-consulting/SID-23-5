A Segmentation might be decomposed as Segments each of them being a combination of Conditions (combined with AND or OR). These Segments are specified thanks to the SegmentSpecification.<br/><br/>Segmentation might be defined so that segments might be disjoined or not, continuous or discontinuous.<br/><br/>For example, the market segmentation of Orange contains several segmentations Pan European, Sub-Saharan Africa…, each of  them being decomposed as combined conditions (Ex: Pan European Segmentation decomposed as Mobile reluctant, Basic Communicators, Pragmatics…segments).<br/>
