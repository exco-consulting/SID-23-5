<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A Segment is the result of applying a SegmentSpecification when a triggeringEvent occurs.<br/><br/>The validity period is calculated according to the triggering event.<br/><br/>The Segment classifies RootEntity’s instances for which the CriterionCondition is “True” when it is built.<br/><br/>The attribute respectsThreshold specifies if the Segment respects the lower and upper size defined by the SegmentSpecification.<br/><br/>A Segment might be composite. A CompositeSegment corresponds to the sum of the composed Segments.<br/>
