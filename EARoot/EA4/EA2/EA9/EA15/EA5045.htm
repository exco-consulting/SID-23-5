A CriterionCondition specifies a condition expressed using Criteria.<br/><br/>A CriterionCondition might be Atomic or Composite.<br/><br/>Each AtomicCriterionCondition specifies a condition on a Criterion such as “If Age #gt; 36”.<br/><br/>Each CompositeCriterionCondition combines two or more AtomicCriterionConditions specifying if all the AtomicCriterionConditions must be True (AND) or if at least one of them must be True (OR).<br/>
