A SegmentationSpecification specifies rules (CriterionCondition) to divide a large set of Entity’s instances into subsets according to criteria. These subsets are called segments.<br/><br/>For example, Market segmentation divides a broad target market into subsets of consumers, businesses, or countries that have, or are perceived to have, common needs, interests, and priorities, and then designing and implementing strategies to target them.<br/><br/>We mainly consider Parties’ segmentation, but we could also segment other concepts such as InstalledResources, Projects…<br/><br/>For example, we could define rules to identify InstalledResources that have a high level of risk of failure.<br/><br/>The SegmentationSpec is evaluated to produce the corresponding Segmentation according to a triggering event. It might be “one shot”, a periodicity or at any update of criteria.<br/>
