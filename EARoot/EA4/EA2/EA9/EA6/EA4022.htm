Figure CM.03 - Types of Demand Entities diagram
Similar to types (subclasses) of Capacity, there are types of CapacityDemand as shown in the following Figure. An example of each, Capacity and CapacityDemand will be described further in the next section.<br/>The different types (subclasses) of CapacityDemand can be related to one another. For example, ServiceCapacityDemand may be used to calculate ResourceCapacityDemand. Or, demand could be aggregated. The CapacityDemandRelationship entity provides support for these and other inter-demand relationships.<br/>
