<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
Represents information that characterizes a workflow. Workflows can be typified by the way they are triggered (event-driven or rules-driven etc.), how they are structured (sequential, non-sequential, etc.), or based on specific objectives (decision control, data ingestion, etc.).<br/>
