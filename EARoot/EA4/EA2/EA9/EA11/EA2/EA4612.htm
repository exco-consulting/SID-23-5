<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
This is a concrete base class for realizing the "event-condition-passaction-failaction" semantics that form a policy rule. The semantics of this rule are that the rule is evaluated when an event occurs. If the condition clause is satisfied, then the pass-action clause will be executed (otherwise, the fail-action clause will be executed).<br/>PolicyRules may be nested within PolicyRules. This is often needed in networking (e.g., bandwidth allocation). However, their execution semantics are different than if a PolicyGroup is contained by a PolicyRule.<br/>
