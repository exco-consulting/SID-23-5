Notes: This is an unsigned integer attribute that defines the relative position of this action in relation to the overall sequence of actions that are associated with a given PolicyRule. Smaller integers indicate earlier positions in the sequence. The value "0" has the special semantics of "don't care".<br/><br/>This value is not unique. Thus, if two or more attributes have the same value, they may be performed in any order, but they must all be performed at the appropriate place in the overall sequence.<br/>
