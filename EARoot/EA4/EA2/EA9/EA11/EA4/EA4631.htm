The ServiceManagementPolicy class defines the particular policies that are used to define how different aspects of the Service are managed and maintained.<br/><br/>For example, assume that a Service is made up of several sub-Services. One of these sub-Services might require multiple PartyRoles to approve changes to its configuration, whereas another sub-Service might not require any approvals to change its configuration.<br/>
