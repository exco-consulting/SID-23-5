A PolicyExecutionPoint is an entity that is used to execute a prescribed set of PolicyActions on a set of PolicyTargets.<br/><br/>The combination of a PolicyExecutionPoint and a PolicyEnforcementPoint enable the act of executing a decision (made by a PolicyDecisionPoint) to be separated from the act of ensuring that the executing actions were performed correctly, and had the desired results.<br/>
