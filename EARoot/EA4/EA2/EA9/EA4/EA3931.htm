Figure BI.10 - Relationship Between Business Interaction and Location diagram
During the interaction, Bob Smith may also provide information about the location (referred to as a Location or Place in the SID Model) of his cable modem.  Therefore, as shown in the figure below, a business interaction also can involve one or more Locations.  In the SID model, locations, such as addresses, geographic areas, and geographic coordinates are collectively called Locations.<br/>
