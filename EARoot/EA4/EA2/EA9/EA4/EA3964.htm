<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A communication that informs about something that has or will happen. A Notification is typically one-sided, in that no Response is expected. A Notification can be created as the result of a Request; for example, a Trouble Report may result in Notifications being sent to affected Parties. A Notification is a type of Business Interaction.<br/>
