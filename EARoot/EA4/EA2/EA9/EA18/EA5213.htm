BusinessRule ABE represents a set of information that captures the definition of instructions to determine how a process (task/activity/workflow/etc.) is performed. <br/>Business rules guide operations in an organization as they determine how processes should be executed to maintain the organization's integrity. Business rules are essential for business process models (workflows) and input to have complete conceptional modeling during requirement elicitation. <br/>In a model-driven domain, modeling activities and workflows without modeling business rules limit the degree of flexibility in automating operations. In a zero-touch operation, the ability to model information using machine-based platforms requires capturing all the entities at play. BusinessRule ABE is one such primary entity that is represented by this contribution.<br/>
