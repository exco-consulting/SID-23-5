Shopping Cart entity is used for the temporarily selection and reservation of product offerings in e-commerce and retail purchase. Shopping cart supports purchase of both tangible and intangible goods and service (e.g. handset, telecom network service). The charge includes the one-off<br/><br/>fee such as the fee for handset and the recurring fee such as the fee of a network service.<br/><br/>It is mainly used in case of unknown customer with only a ContactMedium instead of a Party and PartyRole with their ContactMedium.<br/>
