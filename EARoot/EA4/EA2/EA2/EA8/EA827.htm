Balance-level rule information constraining the application of BillingAccountBalance. It could describe the sharing rule, by which BillingAccountBalance could be shared between different kinds of FinancialCharge which is related to different customer’s product charge. It could emphasize associated BillingAccountBalance is only applicable for certain usage, for example only applicable for local phone call or domestic flow or intra-PLMN SMS.  BalanceUsageRule also includes priority and other kinds of rules limits the application of BillingAccountBalance.<br/>
