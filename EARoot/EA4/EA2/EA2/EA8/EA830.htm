<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A CustomerBillingAccount is an arrangement that a Customer has with an enterprise (such as a communication service provider) through which the enterprise collects AppliedPartyBillingRate (i.e. charges) to structure bills. It also collects payments for Products that are supplied by the enterprise to the Customer. The AppliedPartyBillingRates are invoiced to the BillingAccount via CustomerBills.<br/>
