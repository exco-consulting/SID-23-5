AppliedCustomerBillingProductRecurringCharge represents an amount, usually of money, for which an Individual or Organization is financially liable over recurring period (e.g. monthly) for the right to use a Product. For example, $15/month for basic TV channel access, $10/month for renting a home gateway, $3/month for extended warranty for an iPhone.<br/>
