A BillingAccount may be written off for two main reasons:<br/><br/>      • When the dunning procedures failed to recover the debt, the bad debt must be written off and reported as such to the General Ledger<br/><br/>      • When a contract terminates with a “minimal” credit or debit balance (smaller than a threshold reflecting the cost of the collection process).<br/><br/>The DunningWriteOff corresponds, in a dunning context, to a credit to definitively balance the BillingAccount before being able to close it.<br/>
