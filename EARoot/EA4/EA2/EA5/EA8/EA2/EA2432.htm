DataType
This data type provides three values for severity to cover the three cases of service affecting conditions, non- service affecting conditions and conditions where the service impact is unknown. The structure identifies the specific case of alarm that it applies to using three probable cause identifiers in combination.<br/><br/><br/><br/>See also attached supporting document for further details on the values of the strings probableCause, probableCauseQualifier and nativeProbableCause.<br/>
