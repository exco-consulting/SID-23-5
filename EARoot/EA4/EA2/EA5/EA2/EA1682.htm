The ResourceCapacityDemand represents the amount of ResourceCapacity that is required, planned to be consumed or has been consumed.<br/>The HostingPlatformRequirementSpec requires a ResourceCapacityDemand that in this case specifies the amounts (minimum, maximum…) of capacity required.<br/>A HostingPlatformRequirement is the set of requirements defined by HostingPlatformRequirementSpec which applies to a specific InstalledSoftware. It requires a ResourceCapacityDemand that in this case specifies the amounts (minimum, maximum…) of capacity planned or consumed.<br/><i>Refer to Common Domain guide book for more explanations about the Capacity model.</i><br/>
