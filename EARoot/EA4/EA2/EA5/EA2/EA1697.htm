<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
This class represents the ability to distinguish between different instances of ResourceSpecifications. It represents a particular form or variety of a ResourceSpecification that is different from others or from the original. The form represents differences in attributes, methods, relationships, and/or constraints that characterize this particular ResourceSpecification, but which are not enough to warrant creating a new ResourceSpecification.<br/>
