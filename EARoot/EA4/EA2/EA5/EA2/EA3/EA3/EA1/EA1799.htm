GD (General Deployment)<br/><br/> 4: SV (Special Version for 1 or more customers)<br/><br/> 5: EOE (end of engineering)<br/><br/> 6: EOS (end of sale)<br/><br/> 7: EOL (end of life)<br/><br/> <br/><br/>FCS marks the beginning of the limited deployment phase. EOS means that the SoftwareResourceSpecificatino can no longer be ordered, but will still be supported. EOL means that the SofwareResourceSpecification can no longer be supported, and cannot be ordered.<br/>
