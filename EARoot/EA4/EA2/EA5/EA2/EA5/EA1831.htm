<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A ResourceCandidate is an entity that enables the ResourceSpecifications to a catalog. A ResourceCandidate and its ResourceSpecification may be “published” - made visible - in any number of ResourceCatalogs, or in none.  One ResourceSpecification can be composed of other ResourceSpecifications.<br/>
