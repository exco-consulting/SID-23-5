<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A ResourceFunction is described by a ResourceFunctionSpec specifying the possible configurations.<br/><br/>A ResourceFunction is a function to transform inputs of any nature into outputs of any nature independently from the way it is provided. <br/><br/>Each time a ResourceFunctionSpecification is provided whatever the way it is done (SoftwareSpecification, SoftBlackBoxSpec or PhysicalBlackBox), a ResourceFunction is created with its ConnectionPoints.<br/><br/>The ResourceFunction is provided either by an InstalledSoftware, a SoftBlackBox, or a PhysicalBlackBox. These relationships are exclusive.<br/>
