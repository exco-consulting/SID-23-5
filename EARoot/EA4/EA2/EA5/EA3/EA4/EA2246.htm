<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
This is an abstract base class for describing different types of hardware that constitute a Product. It has two main purposes: (1) to collect common attributes and relationships for all hardware, and (2) to provide a convenient, single point where relationships with other managed objects can be defined.<br/><br/>The HasWarrantyInfo association (not shown) describes warranty information of hardware.<br/>
