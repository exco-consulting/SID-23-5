A Bay is a type of EquipmentHolder that is designed to hold various types of Equipment. It has a logical identifier, usually a component of port AIDs in TL1 based switches.<br/><br/>Note that the logical identifier of a Bay is typically associated with the Device (i.e., the NetworkElement). This means that the Bay IS explicitly a part of the logical model of a network.<br/><br/>A Bay is usually built as a pre-wired hardware assembly that contains a set of shelves and accompanying ancillary Equipment.<br/>
