<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
This is a sub-class of PolicyRule in order to obtain the conditions, events and actions, which help especially for the penalty fees when certain conditions don't apply. The policies' approach is to only be applied and be considered for the number portability itself, depending of the type of the request based on the BusinessInteractionType.<br/>
