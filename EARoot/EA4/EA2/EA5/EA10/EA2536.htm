<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A StockItem represents items used Supply Chain for Stock and Shipping Management.<br/><br/>The codeModel specifies the type of codification used for the stockItemCode.<br/><br/>Two main types exist: EAN (European Article Numbering) or GTIN (Global Trade Item Numbering). A GTIN consists of a GS1 Company Prefix, an Item Reference Number and a Check Digit. <br/><br/>Each of them is further defined by the length of the number such as EAN 13, GTIN-12 (USA / Canada for individual products), GTIN-13 (Rest of World for Individual products), GTIN-14 (Worldwide for Product groupings).<br/>
