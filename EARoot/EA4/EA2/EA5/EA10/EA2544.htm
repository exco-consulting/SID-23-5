A StockItemStockLevel represents the Stock Level of a StockItem stored in a StockLocation and available for SalesChannels.<br/><br/>A StockItemStockLevel might be<br/><br/>        • Available for sale to customers limited to SalesChannels<br/><br/>        • Available for use to build the Network or to provide furniture to employees<br/><br/>        • Returned: StockItems received back from the Customer that must be checked before being available such as rented material returned<br/><br/>        • Damaged: StockItems identified as damaged during a reception from a supplier or returned as damaged by a Customer or Employee<br/>
