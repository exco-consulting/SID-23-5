Figure Pr.32a - Product Price Charge hierarchy diagram
The following diagram shows the ProdPriceCharge hierarchy.<br/><ul>
<li>A <b>UsageProdPriceCharge </b>represents a charge applicable to ProductCharges for a Product.</li><li>A <b>OneTimeProdPriceCharge </b>represents a charge to apply for a Product action that has been done such as creation fee.</li><li>A <b>RecurringProdPriceCharge </b>represents a charge that must be applied on a recurring basis for a Product.</li></ul>
