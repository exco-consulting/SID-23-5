Figure Pr.32b - Product Price Alteration hierarchy diagram
The following diagram shows the ProdPriceAlteration hierarchy.<br/><ul>
<li>A ReplacementProdPriceAlteration represents a new price that replaces a charge for a Product.</li><li>A DiscountProdPriceAlteration represents a discount (a reduction of price) for a Product.</li><li>A TaxProdPriceAlteration is a type of ProdPriceAlteration that applies a TaxDefinition.</li></ul><p/>
