A specific rate that applies to ProductUsages aggregated during a certain period. <br/><br/>For example, all evening minutes are aggregated during one month before being globally rated by a TarifUsageProdOfferPriceCharge 1. And all daytime minutes are aggregated during one month before being globally rated by a TarifUsageProdOfferPriceCharge 2.<br/>
