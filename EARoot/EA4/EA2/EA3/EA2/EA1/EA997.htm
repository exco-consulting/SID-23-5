A RatePlanProductSpec defines criteria to be used to gain special usage tariffs like the period (Ex: evenings and week-ends) or phone number (Ex: friends and family). The ProductOffering packaging a RatePlanProductSpec defines the Usages’ ProductOfferingPrice conditioned by the criteria. A RatePlanProductSpec requires a NetworkProductSpecs.<br/>
