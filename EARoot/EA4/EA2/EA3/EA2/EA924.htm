Figure Pr.04 - Goods Product Spec and Shipping Product Spec diagram
The GoodsProductSpec corresponds to the specification of material goods.<br/>A ShippingProductSpec defines the possible choices of configuration for the shipment of one or several GoodsProductSpecs like the place to ship (Ex: at home or pick-up store), the delivery schedule (Ex: in 1 hour, in 48h)…<br/>A ShippingProductSpecification pre-requires at least one GoodsProductSpecs.<br/>
