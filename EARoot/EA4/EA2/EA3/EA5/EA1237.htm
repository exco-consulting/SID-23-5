<<TMF_BusinessEntity>>
A ProductOrder represents a communication used to procure, update or remove one or many Products in the context of a ProductOffering through all its ProductOrderItems.<br/><br/>One or many PartyRoles might be involved in a ProductOrder / ProductOrderItem such as Distributor, Holder, Buyer or DeliveryDriver.<br/>
