A LoyaltyRule is a type of PolicyRule defined by a LoyaltyProgramProdSpec.<br/><br/>A LoyaltyRule specifies:<br/><br/>• Events triggering the evaluation of the rule (LoyaltyEvent)<br/><br/>   o Ex: Every first of a month, a CustomerProductOrder of Top-Up<br/><br/>• Conditions that have to be checked (LoyaltyCondition)<br/><br/>   o Ex: If the invoice amount is above 100 €, If more than 2 hours of international call<br/><br/>• Actions that should be performed if the condition clause is evaluated to TRUE (LoyaltyAction)<br/><br/>   o Ex: Give 50 SMS free, Credit 10 points on the LoyaltyAccount<br/><br/><br/><br/>Loyalty Rules may result in<br/><br/>• either rewards directly used / usable by the customer<br/><br/>   o Ex: 5% more time on the communications package after 6 months of age, and 10% after one year ...<br/><br/>• or loyalty earn that are gathered on an account to be used to pay later a ProductOffering<br/><br/>   o Ex: 400 points to welcome a subscription then 10 points for every Euro charged. The points collected can be used to buy rewards like a USB stick, 100 SMS…<br/>
