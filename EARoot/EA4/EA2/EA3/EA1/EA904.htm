A ProductManager is a party role which is responsible for the development of products for an organization. Product managers own the business strategy behind a product, specify its functional requirements and generally manage the launch of corresponding ProductOfferings. They coordinate work done by many other functions (like software engineers, data scientists and product designers) and are ultimately responsible for the business success of the product.<br/>
