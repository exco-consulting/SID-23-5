<<TMF_AF>>
The Opportunity Management Aggregate Function enables to collect, qualify the opportunities and supervise their conversion into orders.<br/>Qualify the opportunity first includes to convert the leads into opportunities.<br/>Once a lead is qualified, it is considered as an opportunity, which is worked further down the sales funnel through to either success (sale) or loss/disqualification (no sale).<br/>An opportunity can be generated from many different sources and customer interactions including the result of a targeted marketing campaign. An opportunity is linked with the Offer & Product Catalogue.<br/>All information necessary to make an offer to the Prospective Customer are gathered. Potential customer information is obtained from external sources or from internally generated data.<br/>It could also manage relationships between opportunities and various campaigns.<br/>The opportunity may be further explored with the Prospective Customer (extended interactions) to ensure the customer requirement is understood.<br/>It includes also Proposal Management part which enables the conversion of Opportunities into agreed proposal. <br/>A solution is elaborated, proposed to the Prospective Customer and his acceptation / refusal is collected. There may be a negotiation.<br/>If the Prospective Customer accepts the commercial proposition one or several Order will be generated via the Customer Order Management functions.<br/>
