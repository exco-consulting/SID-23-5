<<TMF_AF>>
Advertising Management AF includes functions supporting planning, executing, and overseeing advertising campaigns to promote products or services. Effective advertising management plays a crucial role in building brand awareness, driving sales, and achieving marketing objectives. Its key activities include setting campaign objectives, identifying target audiences, selecting appropriate advertising channels, creating compelling ad content, budgeting, monitoring campaign performance, and adjusting strategies as needed. The goal is to maximize the reach and impact of advertising while ensuring efficient resource allocation. <br/>
