<<TMF_AF>>
The Framework Agreement Management Aggregate Function consists in defining and managing the agreement that describes the commitments and company features valid for associated customer orders.<br/>It defines a subset of catalogue offers and products which will be marketed to a customer with particular conditions (configurations of product, rates, discounts, SLA such as availability rate, restoration time guaranties, and associated penalties for the CSP).<br/>
