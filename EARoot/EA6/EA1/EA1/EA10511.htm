Campaign Analysis consists in the assessment of the actions and the targeting returns in order to evaluate the generated “added value” (campaign effectiveness, ROI, effectiveness of the channels and teams, cost of the actions (time, budget), relevance or not of the actions carried out) and optimize the next marketing campaigns.<br/>Evaluates the campaign’s performance:<br/>• At the end of the campaign. Proposition take rate will be considered, as well as campaign’s impact on usage, top-up behavior (increase in amount/frequency) and churn rate<br/>• In the long run. E.g., has the retention campaign had a durable impact on churn reduction, or has it triggered opportunistic customer behavior? Has the “stimulate usage” campaign caused cannibalization, or triggered a durable increase in consumption?<br/>
