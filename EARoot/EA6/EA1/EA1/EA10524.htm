Sales Force Management Aggregate Function manages the direct (Customer Service Representatives – CSR – in call centers and shops) and indirect (dealers, resellers, outsourcers…) sales forces. It defines the revenue forecasts, sales representative objectives and enables the follow-up of sales representatives’ forecasts / objectives, sales trends…<br/>It consists in planning their activities, their visits with customers… as well as some indicators about their efficiency in achieving the goals of a marketing campaign. It encompasses the objectives management.<br/>
