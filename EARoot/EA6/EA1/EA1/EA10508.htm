The Marketing Campaign Execution function consists in executing and driving marketing campaign through the different channels. <br/><br/>Campaign execution is launched by performing interactions with customers or prospects and may include scripts execution. It collects answers through different media.<br/>It takes in input the campaign brief, the support to use, script if necessary, and the customer target list or criteria from the campaign design function. <br/>Example of criteria: send specific notifications or alerts (promotions, advertising, …) when a customer (/prospect/user) enters or leaves a designated area (based on geofencing infrastructure – and customer opt-in)<br/><br/>The campaign execution can involve: <br/>• distribution of marketing collateral like leafleting, displays at events, inserts in newspapers, television and other media advertising and magazines or direct marketing through store.<br/>• a voice or text messaging (SMS, mail, call …) for a targeted customer list.<br/>• a document push such as coupon, photo or video <br/><br/>Campaign Execution provides the necessary tools to execute and refine the campaign, and based on performance indicators collected, accept refinements to the campaign while still executing.<br/>Campaign test is the first phase of campaign execution (specific waves, limited audience). It’s restricted execution to validate campaign.<br/>
