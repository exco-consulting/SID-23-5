<<TMF_AF>>
The Sales Interlocutors Management Aggregate Function permits to identify CSP and Customer information and organization, in the context of Opportunity Management or Framework Agreement Management. It mainly concerns B2B customers.<br/><br/>It can contain:<br/>• The attribution to the appropriate sales representatives, according to the territories defined by the Workforce Management function<br/>• The identification and the authorization of the customer company employees (to purchase, to add service, to obtain support, ...), <br/>• The way invoices and/or invoicing reports will be handled in the future (for each order, each site of the enterprise, …) via the Billing Administration function,<br/>
