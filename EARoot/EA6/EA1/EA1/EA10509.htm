<<TMF_AF>>
Marketing Campaign Performance Tracking and Analysis provides the necessary functionality to monitor the campaign performance (e.g. performance indicators) as the campaign is being executed. <br/>It permits to continuously optimize campaigns.<br/>This is done by providing a combination of real-time reporting and what-if modeling capabilities to continuously optimize campaigns by taking corrective action-even when campaigns are already underway. <br/>Functionality specifically includes:<br/>• Real-time view into customizable segmentation schemes and key metrics. <br/>• Dashboard views, alerts, and notifications provide marketing organizations with the insight and agility needed to protect and optimize marketing investments. <br/>• Pre-built Marketing Insight reports.<br/>
