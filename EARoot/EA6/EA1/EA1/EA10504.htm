<<TMF_AF>>
The Marketing Campaign Plan Definition Aggregate Function deals with the marketing campaign plan definition to manage communications to the market, prospective and existing customers, to suggest options to them. <br/><ul>
<li>it defines a whole of campaigns from marketing studies and plan, plans these campaigns</li><li>it optimizes the contacts according to campaign prioritization rules such as the potential creation of value of the proposed offer</li></ul>
It concerns mainly:<br/><ul>
<li>The identification of the campaigns that should be executed to reach the business objectives for a specific customer segment and their sequence (campaign name, description, campaign type, objectives, cost, sponsor, planning)</li><li>The contact rules definition with targeting criterion to make sure that commercial pressure on customer is managed (e.g. "no more than 1 SMS per week for customer segment 1")</li></ul><p/>
