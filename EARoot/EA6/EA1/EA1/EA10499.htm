<<TMF_AF>>
The Sales and Sales Force Management Aggregate Function encompasses several functionalities:<br/>• Sales Territory Management enables to structure the sales teams into territories according to several criteria: business segments, strategic accounts, product lines, industries, geographical areas… The territories are used for the lead / opportunity assignation and the sales forecasts.<br/>• Sales Force Management manages the direct (Customer Service Representatives – CSR – in call centers and shops) and indirect (dealers, resellers, outsourcers…) sales forces. It defines the revenue forecasts, sales representative objectives and enables the follow-up of sales representatives’ forecasts / objectives, sales trends…<br/>It consists in planning their activities, their visits with customers… as well as some indicators about their efficiency in achieving the goals of a marketing campaign. It encompasses the objectives management.<br/>
