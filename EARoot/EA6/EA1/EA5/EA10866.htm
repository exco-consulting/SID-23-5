<<TMF_AF>>
The Logistic Availability Aggregate Function allows to:<br/>• Check the availability of tangible products,<br/>• Get the delivery duration,<br/>• Book/release a tangible product.<br/><br/>For this function, the supply chain uses its own stock and delivery duration, but also the stock and delivery duration of its partners.<br/>
