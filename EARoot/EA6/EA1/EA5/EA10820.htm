The Network Performance Management aggregate function ensures the efficiency and reliability of a CSPs network infrastructure. Its responsibilities can be summarized in the following key points:<br/>1. Monitoring Network Health: Continuously track network performance and identify issues.<br/>2. Fault Detection: Quickly detect and diagnose network faults or failures.<br/>3. Performance Optimization: Optimize network configurations and resources for efficiency.<br/>4. Bandwidth Management: Ensure proper allocation of bandwidth to meet user needs.<br/>5. Security Monitoring: Monitor for potential security threats and vulnerabilities.<br/>6. Capacity Planning: Forecast network growth and plan for scalability.<br/>7. Reporting and Analytics: Provide data-driven insights for decision-making.<br/>8. Quality of Service (QoS): Ensure consistent and reliable service quality.<br/>9. Incident Management: Rapidly respond to and resolve network incidents.<br/>10. Compliance: Ensure network operations align with regulatory and organizational standards, such as SLAs.<br/>
