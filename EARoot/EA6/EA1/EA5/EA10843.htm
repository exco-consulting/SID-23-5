A Resource Order describes actions required on resources, and can be initiated in several contexts:<br/>• by a Resource Order Management component to update the equipments required to deliver/activate a CFS, or modify a technical solution element<br/>• by the Supply Chain to answer to the customer order of a goods product<br/>• by the Infrastructure Management process, to install new equipments<br/>• by the Service & Resource Assurance process to diagnose or resolve a trouble<br/><br/>
