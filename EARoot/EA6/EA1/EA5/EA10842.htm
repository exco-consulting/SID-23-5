<<TMF_AF>>
The Resource Order Completion Aggregate Function permits to enrich the Resource Order with information needed by the equipment, but which are not part of the description of the asked action and haven’t to be known by the requester of this action. <br/>Example: the name of the holder or user may be needed by a service platform to deliver a CFS.<br/>
