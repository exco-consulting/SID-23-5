Resource Capability Specification Management Aggregate Function manages Resource Capability Specifications for all Resource Specifications. The capability specifications represent the general, common and invariant characteristics of resource that may be realized in more than one type of specific resource. Examples of capability are Layer2, Data, radio and Transport.<br/>
