<<TMF_AF>>
The Resource Specification Management Aggregated Function functions enable to create and update the catalogue at Resource level, for all types of resources (ResourceSpecification):<br/>• Physical Resources (ex: cable, passive equipment, …)<br/>• Logical Resources (ex: phone number, IP address, APPS such as Orange TV, Orange Maps, …)<br/>• Compound Resources (ex: DSLAM, box, …)<br/>It manages the links with the Business Partner Product Catalogue for Resources specification that correspond to equipment rented to a supplier (devices, network equipment, hardware & software, …).<br/>It also manages the links to the Skill catalogue to identify for each type of resource – or of operation on a resource – which type of skill is necessary to make the intervention.<br/>
