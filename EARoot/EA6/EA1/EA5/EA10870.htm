<<TMF_AF>>
Work Order Tracking and Business Value Development Aggregate Function tracks and manages the events and the lifecycle related to Work Orders, and assures that they are assigned, coordinated and escalating as needed.<br/><br/>It is especially in charge of initiating re-assignments as needed, based on changing priorities, technician progress on other job assignments, etc.<br/>
