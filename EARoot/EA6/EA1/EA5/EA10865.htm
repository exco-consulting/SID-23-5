<<TMF_AF>>
The Stock Movements Management Aggregate Function manages items movements (i.e. shipment). It includes transfers and reallocations within the organization or to partners.<br/>It's in charge of shipment realization and follow-up including when it is directly done by a business partner.<br/>Examples: This function is used when devices are delivered in shop, or when an equipment is replaced by a technician during an intervention.<br/>Stock Movements Management also manages the stock of documentation and type of documentation (Commercial catalogue, flyers etc.).<br/><br/>
