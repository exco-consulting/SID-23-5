<<TMF_AF>>
The Resource Deployment and Retirement Aggregate Function is responsible for implementing the resource strategy high level plan and develop the resource infrastructure (network, PFS, IT...). The Resource Deployment and Retirement function manages the deployment constraints like area limitation (for test or for marketing purposes), progressive deployment following precise milestones… These constrains may be provided from the service level.<br/><br/>The resource deployment and retirement can be responsible to manage constraints (like legal constraints or the need of validation by authorities) and notify other companies, local authorities or governments of the effective deployment or retirement of resources like the implementation of FTTH technology in a building.<br/><br/>The Resource Installed Base is updated and the resources are pre-configured and pre-activated if needed, for example for non-customer specific parameters, via the Resource Order Initialization and Installed Resource Management functions.<br/>
