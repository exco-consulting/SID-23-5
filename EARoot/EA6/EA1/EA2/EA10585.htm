The Customer Order Completion function enables to complete and finalize the Customer Order, after its validation with the requester and before triggering its delivery, with collect of Customer Data.<br/>The complements could concern customer order links with actors, dates, appointment, address, billing account.<br/><br/>The final validation of the Customer Order can trigger the generation of documents such as:<br/>• a contract document, summing up the customer order, and the standard and specific sales terms<br/>• a simplified identification mean such as QR / bar code, and its sending to the customer, by MMS, email, … to ease later interactions and identification of the order. Example: to pick up in store a goods product ordered from web portal or mobile apps, the customer just needs to present the sent QR / bar code.<br/>
