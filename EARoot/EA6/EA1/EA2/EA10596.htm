<<TMF_AF>>
The Billing Account administration Aggregate Function manages all the information related to the billing account, needed to produce an invoice and agreed with the customer such as :<br/>• The associated invoicing group (linked to the periodicity and the date of the invoice production),<br/>• Perimeter of installed contracts and products charged to the billing account, and for which any type of charges can be collected (one time charges, recurring charges or usage charges),<br/>• Parties and party roles concerned by the billing process, such as Holder, Payer, Bill receiver, etc.,<br/>• Invoice structure and presentation rules,<br/>• And possibly default payment means for automatic generation of payments.<br/>
