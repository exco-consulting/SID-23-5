<<TMF_AF>>
The Invoicing Aggregate Function comprises two steps: bill composition (i.e. sorting and assembly of bill items) and bill production. <br/>It enables either physical or electronic invoices, through the formatting and generation of final form (paper, CD-ROM, pdf …) and delivery (sending by e-mail, post or displaying through a web self-care portal). <br/>On electronic format, the invoice can include tools enabling consumption analysis for corporate needs for instance.<br/>
