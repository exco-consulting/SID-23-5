<<TMF_AF>>
The Customer Order Validation Aggregate Function permits to validate the Customer Order, or the cancellation of a Customer Order, with the requester, according to Offer and Product catalogue and installed base (see Customer Order Eligibility function), and to provisioning rules and delays, after tariff calculation with possibly Framework agreement special conditions (see Tariff calculation and rating function).<br/>
