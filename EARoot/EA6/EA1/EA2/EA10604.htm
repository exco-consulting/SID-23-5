<<TMF_AF>>
The Risk Mitigation Definition Aggregate Function is meant to define an array of actions, related to triggering conditions such as threshold(s) of billable amounts defined according to risk level, to avoid bill shock.<br/>It also defines Customer criteria (such as VIP) to identify the corresponding prevention scenario to be applied.<br/>
