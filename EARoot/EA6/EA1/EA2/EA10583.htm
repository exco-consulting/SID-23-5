<<TMF_AF>>
The Customer Order Commercial Eligibility Aggregate Function validates that the Offer & products specified on the Customer Order, are available and feasible from a commercial and functional point of view.<br/>It includes:<br/>• Commercial Eligibility with commercial compatibility with the already customer installed Offers and with customer profile (age, location, …) <br/>• Functional Eligibility with functional compatibility with the already customer installed Products.<br/>• Customer Credit Eligibility check, considering the payment history and the credit scores for a Customer, only the predictive credit score for a Prospective Customer.<br/>The technical feasibility at the product level is verified relying on the Service Availability function.<br/>
