<<TMF_AF>>
Customer Order Repository Management Aggregate Function tracks and manages the events and the lifecycle related to the Customer Order and to its items (e.g. : Order lines).<br/>When the Customer Order is completed, this function publishes the event “Order Completed”. This event usually triggers the Customer Order Orchestration function to deliver the Customer Order.<br/>It gathers product items delivery events from Customer Order Orchestration and manages related Customer Order lifecycle. It also manages these statuses in case of Order cancellation.<br/>Depending on the Customer Order (or any of its elements) events, and on the implemented business rules, this function can decide to notify automatically the customer of the Customer Order improvement. The notification itself can be made by SMS / email / other medium and is done by the functions of the Welcome and Interaction Aggregate Function.<br/>
