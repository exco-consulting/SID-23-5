<<TMF_AF>>
The Risk Mitigation Measures Enforcement Aggregate Function enforces the Risk Mitigation  scenarios previously defined by the Customer Risk Mitigation Definition, that is:<br/>• Detection of billable amounts threshold (according to billing account owner’s risk profile and related scenario),<br/>• Triggering of actions (e.g.: sending alert SMS, triggering product barring…) via dedicated functions.<br/>
