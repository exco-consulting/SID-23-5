<<TMF_AF>>
The Workforce Schedule Management Aggregate Function permits to initiate and update staff calendars and availability per type of activity and possible carrying out models – at team and individual levels – according to criteria and rules.<br/><br/>The workload management schedule is first concerned by future requirements and is limited to capacity management - for internal teams and subcontractors. It uses mid-term activity forecasts.<br/>The workload management schedule is then updated and detailed at teams and - if necessary - individuals levels to become operational (ex: for the next 2 weeks).<br/><br/>The scheduled can be adapted depending on Workforce Activity Tracking information. Adaptation may consist in allowing dispatching of staff from other geographical area or to other activities, adapt staff allocation per type of activity, hiring temporary manpower, increase subcontracting volume, …<br/>
