<<TMF_AF>>
The Treasury Management Aggregate Function consists in managing short or medium term cash-flow forecasts, financial investments for cash surplus as well as cash funding for company requirements.<br/>It embeds the following sub-functions:<br/>• daily cash flow position delivery, at short-term and medium-term<br/>• cash flow budget management to forecast cash requirements or surplus<br/>• certification and transmission of settlements or debit orders towards banks by the means of SWIFT or EBICS messages<br/>• cash flows transfers accounting<br/>• bank reconciliation<br/>• foreign exchange rates management.<br/>
