<<TMF_AF>>
Human Resource Social Management Aggregate Function groups functions covering all the operations and data related to social management.<br/>It defines the policy for employee’s relations, health and safety, and corporate social responsibility. It supports managers in their implementation and control results.<br/>Each company / country develops its own approach in employee’s relations, health and safety, work time and corporate social responsibility in coherence with group policies and local legal and mandatory requirements and agreements.<br/>
