<<TMF_AF>>
The Training Management Aggregate Function addresses and manages the training functions in its globality from training elaboration and delivery to course assessment after course attending.<br/>It permits to manage employee inscription to course session, courses assessment, etc… <br/><br/>It embeds the following sub-functions:<br/>• “Training plan management”, <br/>• “Training catalogue management”, <br/>• “Training Course session inscription management”,<br/>• External training purchasing<br/>• Training delivery management.<br/>
