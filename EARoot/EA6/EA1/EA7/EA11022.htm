Human Resource Human Capital Management Aggregate Function groups functions covering all the operations and data related to human resource and competencies development.<br/>It covers, on one hand, the internal and external processes of recruitment, and, on the other hand, the processes contributing in the management of the performance and competencies which aims to improve individual performance and, by this way, to insure the achievement of Orange group's strategic business goals.<br/>
