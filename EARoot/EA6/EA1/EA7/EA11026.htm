<<TMF_AF>>
The General Ledger Aggregate Function provides a central repository for all financial transactions and statistical information. It is at the core of accountancy. In general, it permits to define a multi-company, multi-divisional, multi-currency ledger.<br/>• Natural accounts management (e.g. Account 401 to aggregate all the auxiliaries suppliers 401xxx)<br/>• Posting in Control Accounts + other accounts<br/>• Enter journals, upload journals<br/>• Reversal of journals (i.e.: accruals)<br/>• Open and Close GL Periods<br/>• Foreign currency Revaluation and Translation<br/>• Cost and Revenue breakdown<br/>• Reporting and analysis.<br/>
