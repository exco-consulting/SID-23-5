<<TMF_AF>>
The Workforce Activity Tracking Aggregate Function permits on-the-fly analysis of work orders management and workforce activity – including subcontractor's activities. <br/><br/>It produces reports and KPI such as <br/>• workload per type of activity, per team or individual<br/>• wait of intervention per type of activity<br/>• short term forecasts of activity, based on effective production <br/>• etc.<br/>It permits to measure current efficiency and to identify over or under loaded calendars (ex: increase of field interventions on a specific area after storm damage).<br/><br/>These information can help to adapt the short term workforce calendars - through the dedicated function.<br/>
