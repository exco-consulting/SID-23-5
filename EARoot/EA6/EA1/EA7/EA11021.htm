The Human Resource Administration, Compensation and Benefits Management Aggregate Function groups functions in charge of<br/>1. processing and managing records and employee’s data, ensuring they are accurate and up-to-date, corresponding and taking into account any changes resulting from management decisions, legal and regulatory requirements and personal circumstances,<br/>2. managing the individual Compensation and Benefits calculation, giving the instructions for the payroll process.<br/>Note that for some countries (UK), the Information System must provide functions for pension funds and social security management.<br/>
