<<TMF_AF>>
The Asset Management Aggregate Function mainly enables one to:<br/>• manage all the resources which are used and shared within the company, namely the physical and financial assets deployed in the enterprise<br/>• optimize the expected savings linked to these all along their lifecycle from their purchase and setup until their total depreciation or amortization.<br/><br/>This Aggregate Function embeds three functions:<br/>• Corporate Real Estate Management<br/>• Vehicle Fleet Management<br/>• Intellectual Property Management<br/><br/>This Aggregate Function DOESN'T manage the Tangible and Intangible Products sold to the CUSTOMERS, only those used within the enterprise for its own needs.<br/>
