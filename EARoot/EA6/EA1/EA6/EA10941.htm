The Business Partner Management aggregate function functions enable the creation and updates of Business Partner information such as name, address, organization, site location, interlocutors and interlocutor roles (legal representatives). <br/>The term “Business Partner” is used in the broad sense: dealer, reseller, wholesales, content provider (e.g. TV on XDSL), other network operator for interconnection or roaming purpose, Logistic Operator, partner, service provider for the technical support and IT projects, etc.<br/>When the CSP decides to work with a Business Partner, he is referenced and typified (Supplier or Supplier and Partner, or with regard to the contents of purchases). <br/>It also manages supplier lists, preferred suppliers lists and also suppliers' black list, with information such as name, address, etc.<br/>
