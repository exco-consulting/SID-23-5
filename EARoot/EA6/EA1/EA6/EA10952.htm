<<TMF_AF>>
The Settlement Management Aggregate Function is necessary when enterprises want to avoid multiple two-way financial flows, for example when : <br/>• the agreement with the supplier includes revenue sharing (ex : N° contact)<br/>• and/or when the CSP sells on behalf and collects the money for the partner. <br/>It permits invoices aggregation per partner (received invoices and produced invoices) using a settlement account. <br/>According to the balance amount of the settlement account, a payment is sent or requested by the CSP to the partner.  <br/><br/>Notice: Settlement notes and invoice notes are both sent to the partner even when only one financial transfer is performed.<br/>
