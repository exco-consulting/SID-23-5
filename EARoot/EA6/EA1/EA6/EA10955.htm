<<TMF_AF>>
The Business Partner Role Repository Management Aggregate Function enables to identify the relevant roles associated with a Party during the commercial relationship with a Supplier. <br/>The term “Supplier” is used in the broad sense: dealer, reseller, wholesales, content provider (e.g. TV on XDSL), other network operator for interconnection or roaming purpose, Logistic Operator, partner, service provider for the technical support and IT projects, …<br/>When we decide to work with a supplier, he is referenced and typified (Supplier or Supplier and Partner, or with regard to the contents of purchases). Partner is a particular typology of Supplier.<br/><br/>The Business Partner Repository Management is also in charge of gathering financial information related to potential partners, partner groups and hierarchies.<br/>
