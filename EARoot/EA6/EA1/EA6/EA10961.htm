<<TMF_AF>>
The Supplier Bill Calculation Aggregate Function prepares all the bill items necessary for the invoicing process on behalf of a partner. <br/>It collects, and eventually aggregates, the relevant results of the rating function, for the partner and for a dedicated period. <br/>It can also involve bill items coming from other providers.<br/>It can calculate penalties or bonus depending on global amounts (of revenue, of usage types, …) or apply complex tax rules.<br/>
