<<TMF_AF>>
Business Partner Rating and Revenue Sharing Aggregate Function is in charge of:<br/><ul>
<li>Business Partner Revenue Sharing Identification that identifies the relevant pricing rules (tariffs, discounts and penalties) to be applied according to Revenue Sharing Agreement.</li><li>Business Partner Revenue Sharing Enforcement that applies revenue sharing pricing rules to calculate what the CSP owes to the Business Partner and what is owed by the Business Partner to the CSP.</li></ul>
<ul>
<li>And Business Partner Settlement Management that calculates settlements amounts according to what the CSP owes to the Business Partner (for Offers bought to the BP and for Revenue Sharing) and what is owed by the Business Partner to the CSP (for what has been bought by the BP to the CSP and for Revenue sharing).</li></ul>
<br/>It is done for any type of charges:<br/><ul>
<li> recurring charges</li><li> one-time charges (installation fees,  …)</li><li> usage charges, based on unitary product usage records (CDR) or on previously aggregated usage records</li></ul>
<br/>This information can be necessary to validate the supplier invoice the CSP will receive, or to prepare an invoice on behalf of the partner, for example in case of revenue sharing (ex : settlement of 10% of the revenue billed by the CSP to his customers, for a set of contents, to his supplier).<br/>
