<<TMF_AF>>
The main goal of the Business Partner Performance Evaluation Aggregate Function is to reduce purchase risk and maximize the overall value for the purchaser. It typically involves evaluation, at a minimum, business partner quality, cost competitiveness, potential delivery performance and technological capability. Some of the other criteria used in the preliminary evaluation of business partners include financial risk analysis, evaluation of previous performance, and evaluation of business partner provided information.<br/>Other criteria on the ethics and the environment can be also taken into account.<br/>
