<<TMF_AF>>
The Collect and Qualify Business Partner Information Aggregate Function concerns the management of our knowledge of the Business Partner to keep or produce an up-to-date, accurate and legally compliant Business Partner information view.<br/>It will incorporate into the Business Partner Information view, all relevant information gathered through all contacts with the Business Partner and in particular his field of activity.<br/>Business Partner Information from other sources may be gathered, which will build a better knowledge of the supplier.<br/>
