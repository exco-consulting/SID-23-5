<<TMF_AF>>
The Business Partner Product Repository Management Aggregate Function allows to manage the configuration and statuses of the offers and products ordered to the Business Partner, according to its Offer and Product catalogue descriptions and rules. It also manages relationships between Products bought to the Business Partner with the CSP's different level of installed base (Product, Know-How a.k.a CFS, Technical Solution a.k.a RFS and Resource).<br/><br/>This Aggregate Function allows:<br/>• The Order Capture process to instantiate or update offers and products ordered to the Business Partner, whatever their type (network product, bundle, device, …) or their marketing mode (rented, sold, …), with their configuration, their tariffs and discounts, and their status (initialized with a creation order)<br/>• The Order Delivery to update Offer and Product installed base status<br/>• Any process to read Offer and Product installed base (subscribed offers, configuration of installed products, installed tariffs and discount, statuses, …).<br/>
