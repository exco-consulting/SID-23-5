The Business Partner Agreement Management Aggregate Function includes supplier products and offers description, including prices, negotiated with the supplier according to the RFP (RFQ) content.<br/>These agreements can be more or less complex and include:<br/>• Purchases of materials and/or service deliveries, with prices depending on quantity, volume discounts or penalties, etc.<br/>• Revenue sharing conditions, depending on end-customers usages for example.<br/><br/>
