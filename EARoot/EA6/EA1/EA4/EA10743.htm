<<TMF_AF>>
The Service Deployment and Retirement Aggregate Function is responsible for implementing the service strategy high level plan and develop the service infrastructure that will be used to provide the service (through products) to the customers at large (mass market, enterprise, to third parties/partners...) The Service Deployment and Retirement function manages the deployment constraints like area limitation (for test or for marketing purposes), progressive deployment following precise milestones<br/><br/>The service deployment and retirement can be responsible to manage constraints (like legal constraints or the need of a validation by authorities) and notify other companies, local authorities or governments of the effective deployment or retirement of a service.<br/><br/>Based on the resources developed in the Resource Installed Base, the Service Installed Base is updated and pre-configured if needed, for example for generic services with no specific customer configuration.<br/>
