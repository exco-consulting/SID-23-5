<<TMF_AF>>
The Service Order Initialization Aggregate Function permits to:<br/>• Identify the CFS specification and configuration related to the ordered product, in a context of customer order delivery<br/>• Check if a corresponding CFS is operational in the service repository, and so determine the operation at CFS level (creation or modification)<br/>• Initiate a service (CFS) order with these information<br/>• Eventually group in the same service order several ordered product, based on the same CFS specification, and/or identified to be delivered at the same time by the Customer Order Orchestration.<br/><br/>This function can also permit to initialise CFS Orders for operator internal needs, for example to change (part of) a technical solution and migrate operational CFS on the new solution elements (ex: VOIP H323 -#gt; VOIP SIP).<br/>
