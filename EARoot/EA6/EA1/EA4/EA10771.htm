<<TMF_AF>>
The Technical Solution Policy Management Aggregate Function enables to define - and to check consistency of - potentially complex rules to automate the choice of the technical solution for a know-how – or parts of the technical solution.<br/><br/>Criteria can be of various nature and combined :<br/>• geographical<br/>• depending on the chosen characteristics values of the know-how<br/>• depending on other products / know-hows of the customer – installed or ordered<br/>• depending on the current state of Service Platforms, Network equipment (occupation rate, load balancing, … )<br/>• depending on Supplier/Partner contracts (volume discounts, …)<br/>
