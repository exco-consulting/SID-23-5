<<TMF_AF>>
The Service Repository Management Aggregate Function stores and maintains the instances of know-hows (CustomerFacingServices) corresponding to products, ordered by customers, their configuration and links between them (ex: relies on).<br/><br/>It also describes and maintains, for each installed know-how (or service), the links with the chosen technical solution (ResourceFacingServiceSpec and corresponding Resources) which permit to manage the change of a technical solution – or part of it – for instances of know-how (example: new generation of Service Platform to deploy).<br/>
