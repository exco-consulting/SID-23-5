<<TMF_AF>>
Service Order Repository Management Aggregate Function tracks and manages the events and the lifecycle related to the Service (CFS) Order and to its items (e.g.: service order lines).<br/>It gathers Service Order items delivery events from Service Order Orchestration and manages related Service Order and CFS lifecycles (via the Service Management Aggregate Function).<br/>
