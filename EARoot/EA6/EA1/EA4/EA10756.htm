The Service Order Orchestration function provides workflow and orchestration capabilities for a dedicated Service (CFS) Order.<br/>Orchestration is needed when:<br/>• the technical solution includes the update of the operator Installed Resources and the purchase of a partner product (ex: local loop purchase)<br/>• a work order is necessary at the delivery address or somewhere in the operator network<br/>• part of the delivery process or checks are delegated to another SOM (and factory) (ex : check access or update it)<br/>• contributing or support systems have to be informed (ex : Karma for the box)<br/><br/>Example: to deliver a VOIP service, it will orchestrate actions on Access Network Factory (to check access compatibility), VOIP service platform and CPE.<br/><br/>Service Order Orchestration will also orchestrate and manage dependencies between related Service Order items of Service Order.<br/>
