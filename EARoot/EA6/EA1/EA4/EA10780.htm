<<TMF_AF>>
The Service Usage Guiding Aggregate Function ensures resource usage records aggregation (consolidation, elimination of duplicates) to generate service usage records ("global usage repor" often called Charging Data Record or CDR).<br/>Each service usage record is associated to the installed service that generated it (ex: mobile line service).<br/>
