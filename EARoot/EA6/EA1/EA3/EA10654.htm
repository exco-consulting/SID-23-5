The Product Usage Analysis Aggregate Function comprises:<br/>• Analysis of the customers' usages with identification and quantification of the products little used and even not used.<br/>• Forecast of the usages with predictions of the product uses with a chronological modeling of the tendencies and possible breaks of consumption, in order to be compliant with the company strategy (impacts: financial, marketing, trade, network…).<br/>
