<<TMF_AF>>
The Product Specification & Offering Management Aggregate Function Functions enable to create and update the catalog. This function covers the impact on the offer and product catalogue of new offers and products launches, modifications and withdrawals. This function manages the lifecycle of offers and products and includes the various possibilities of offer or product search and browse (wizard, multicriterion search, category browse…).<br/>
