<<TMF_AF>>
The Product Performance Reporting Aggregate Function provides special report requested by customer. Some customers may request from their telecommunication operator, some specific reports, for example, concerning their consumption on a particular site, the quality of service on a particular technology. Usually, this kind of service is supported by operators for major corporate customers.<br/>
