Product Specification & Offering Strategy Definition & Analysis defines the overall structure of the offers and products portfolios to be used across the enterprise. It is established, from the Offer and Product analysis, in respect with the strategic business direction and covers new offers and products as well as existing offers and products. This Aggregated Function also groups functions related to Product Specification & Offering Strategy Analysis conduct.<br/>
