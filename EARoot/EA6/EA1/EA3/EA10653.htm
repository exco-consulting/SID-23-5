<<TMF_AF>>
The Product Repository Management Aggregate Function allows to manage the configuration and statuses of the offers and products owned by a customer, according to Offer and Product catalogue descriptions and rules.<br/><i>Note: the customer owning the Product might be the CSP when it represents a Product bought by the CSP to a Supplier.</i><br/><br/>This function also allows:<br/>•  to instantiate or update offers and products ordered by the customer, whatever their type (network product, bundle, device, …) or their marketing mode (rented, sold, …), with their configuration, their tariffs and discounts, and their status (initialized with a creation order)<br/>• to update Offer and Product installed base status<br/>•  to search and read  Product installed base (subscribed offers, configuration of installed products, installed tariffs and discount, statuses, …).<br/>It also includes the check of configuration rules such as prerequisite or mandatory characteristic for each Product submitted.<br/>
