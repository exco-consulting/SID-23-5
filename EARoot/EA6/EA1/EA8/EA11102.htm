<<TMF_AF>>
The Content Delivery AF groups functions that ensuring the effective and efficient dissemination of digital content to end-users. Their responsibilities include:<br/>1. Content Distribution: Efficiently delivering digital content to end-users across various platforms and devices (Source: "Content Delivery Network (CDN)" - TechTarget).<br/>2. Load Balancing: Ensuring even distribution of network traffic to prevent overloads and improve performance.<br/>3. Caching Management: Implementing cache strategies to reduce load times and optimize content delivery.<br/>4. Traffic Routing: Directing user requests to the nearest server for faster content access.<br/>5. Security: Protecting content and networks from cyber threats and DDoS attacks .<br/>6. Quality of Service: Maintaining high-quality content delivery, including minimizing latency and packet loss <br/>7. Scalability: Ensuring the system can handle increased demand without performance degradation<br/>8. Monitoring and Analytics: Constantly tracking content delivery performance and analyzing data for optimization.<br/>9. Content Optimization: Compressing, transcoding, or adapting content for better delivery to various devices.<br/>
