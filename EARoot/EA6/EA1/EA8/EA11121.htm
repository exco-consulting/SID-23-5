The Business Intelligence aggregate function groups functions that leverage data to inform decision-making and drive strategic insights. Its responsibilities include:<br/>1. Data Gathering: Collecting and aggregating data from various sources.<br/>2. Data Analysis: Analyzing data to identify trends, patterns, and key insights.<br/>3. Reporting: Creating reports and dashboards for stakeholders.<br/>4. Forecasting: Using historical data to predict future trends.<br/>5. Data Visualization: Presenting data in a visually understandable format.<br/>6. Performance Metrics: Developing KPIs to measure business success.<br/>7. Competitive Analysis: Evaluating the organization's position in the market.<br/>8. Decision Support: Providing data-driven insights for informed decision-making.<br/>
