<<TMF_AF>>
The Document Management Aggregate Function handles creation and management of electronic documents.<br/>Documents managed by an EDM (Electronic Document Management) system are both working documents, which can be modified, and final documents. The sub-function Versioning allows the identification of successive releases of the document. Documents are accessed by the user according to the granted access rights.<br/>Subfunctions :<br/>• Capture and integration in the EDM system (for examples GED, sharepoint, social network, …)<br/>• Retention and disposal<br/>• Access and grant control<br/>• Traceability, security and administration<br/>• Versioning.<br/>
