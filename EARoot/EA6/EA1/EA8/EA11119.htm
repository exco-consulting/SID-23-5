Systems Infrastructure Management AF groups functions responsible for overseeing the foundational technology components critical to an organization's operations. Its key responsibilities include:<br/>1. Infrastructure Planning: Strategically designing and scaling the IT infrastructure to meet business needs.<br/>2. Hardware Procurement: Sourcing, purchasing, and maintaining server, storage, and networking equipment.<br/>3. Software Integration: Ensuring seamless integration of software solutions across the infrastructure.<br/>4. Security Management: Safeguarding systems and data through security measures and protocols.<br/>5. Capacity Optimization: Efficiently utilizing resources to meet performance demands.<br/>6. Disaster Recovery: Developing and testing plans for data and system recovery.<br/>7. Network Management: Managing network connectivity and reliability.<br/>8. Performance Monitoring: Constantly assessing system performance and addressing issues.<br/>9. Vendor Relations: Managing relationships with technology suppliers and service providers.<br/>10. Compliance and Documentation: Ensuring adherence to industry standards and maintaining comprehensive system documentation<br/>
