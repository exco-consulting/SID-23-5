<<TMF_AF>>
Party Repository Management Aggregate Function allows the creation, modification, deletion of a Third Party (induvidual or organization).<br/>For people, it allows to declare their marital status and official proof of identity.<br/>For an organization (company, association, etc.), it manages its corporate name, the description of its internal organization according to several axes (legal, functional, etc.), its business justification (Kbis in France, etc.).<br/>Party Management also includes the management of the life cycle of parties.<br/>
