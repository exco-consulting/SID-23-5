Customer Problem Verification and Closure function is responsible for verifying that the problems have been resolved (end-to-end solution) and to close the cases when the problems are indeed resolved. As part of verification and closure, manual follow-up may be needed with customer service representatives, if a complaint (or commendation) was placed against the individual, which need to be tracked. Appropriate actions must be taken, especially if trends are discovered.<br/>
