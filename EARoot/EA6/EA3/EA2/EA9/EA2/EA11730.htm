<<TMF_Function>>
Billing Cycle Coordination function triggers and coordinates the bill cycle activities required to produce normal bill-cycle-based invoices.  This function is not responsible for performing any of those activities - only triggering, monitoring, and coordinating.  Its responsibilities include:<br/>1) triggering bill cycle activities in proper sequence<br/>2) monitoring progress of bill-cycle activities<br/>3) generating alerts for any activities that are either not completing or failing within required time frames<br/>4) re-starting any stalled bill-cycle activities<br/>4) monitoring performance of bill-cycle activities<br/>5) collecting bill-cycle process information that can be used in reports<br/>
