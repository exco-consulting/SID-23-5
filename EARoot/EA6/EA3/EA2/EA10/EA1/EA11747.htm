Loyalty Program Partnerships Management function supports defining the nature of and terms of partnerships with whom a Service Provider's  (and Business Partners') loyalty programs are realized. This may include defining rewards offered by either party, settlement mechanisms and terms, co-marketing plans and arrangements, as well as any integrations that may be necessary to implement the partnerships.<br/>
