<<TMF_Function>>
Voice Channel Contact Routing function provides the means for a customer to speak to a service representative, including the mechanism to query the customer (e.g. – IVR) on the nature of their request, and routing of the contact to the best available agent with the skills required for the contact and presents to the agent selected call information.<br/>
