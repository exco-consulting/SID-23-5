Customer Treatment Decision Automation provides the functionality to drive decisions regarding Customer treatment and action for each specific situation based on observing what the customer is immediately experiencing. This action can be taken either proactively by reaching out to the customer, or reactively when the customer contacts the Service Provider.<br/>
