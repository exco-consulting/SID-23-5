<<TMF_Function>>
Customer Profile Signature Creation function creates a personalized profile signature based on combined segment, interest and behavior for the individual “customer”  (ex. Soccer Mom, Gold/Silver/Bronze customer, Golfer, Early adopter), and around “content” (ex. Content sharing - College kids + Field Sales). This also includes continuous learning and update of individual ‘Profile Signature’.<br/>
