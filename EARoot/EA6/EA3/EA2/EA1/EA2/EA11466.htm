Loyalty and Retention Management Support function undertakes trend analysis on loyalty and retention, including customer acquisitions and churn. It comprises: <br/><ul>
<li>Follow-up of the churn rate: Development of a report in order to measure by period (periodicity: weekly, monthly, yearly…), </li><li>The number and rate of terminations by product & service, </li><li>The trend of the churn rate according to the previous periods,</li><li>Prediction of the churn (anticipate in order to minimize the attrition): identification of the potential churner profiles from a predictive model analyzing the churner profiles according to the previous periods.</li></ul><p/>
