Loan Transfer function is responsible for overseeing and managing the process of transferring loans between different financial institutions or servicing entities. The supported principal process activities responsibilities include: Coordinating loan transfer activities, Ensuring data accuracy, managing documentation, Adhering to regulatory guidelines, Collaborating with stakeholders, Maintaining records, Monitoring transfer timelines, and Implementing best practices. These responsibilities help facilitate smooth and compliant loan transfers while maintaining accurate records and minimizing operational risks.<br/>
