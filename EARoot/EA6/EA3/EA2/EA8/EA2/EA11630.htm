Bill dispute consists of handling the customer inquiries received through customer service, self care or any other channel about their bills that have not been solved by simple actions (e.g walk customer through their invoice or grant the request when disputed amount is inferior to a threshold). <br/><ul>
<li>The initial customer contact and the disputed amount are recorded.</li></ul><p/>
