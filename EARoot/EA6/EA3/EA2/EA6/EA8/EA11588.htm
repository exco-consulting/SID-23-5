<<TMF_Function>>
Customer Order Cancellation can optionally support Cancel for order completed by Service Order Management (this capability is dependent on the Service Order Management system’s ability to roll back service provisioning). This function assesses the feasibility of order cancellation and the potential charge to the customer. If the cancellation is confirmed, it proceeds with the cancellation.<br/>
