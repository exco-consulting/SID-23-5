<<TMF_Function>>
The Automatic Service Reconciliation Function directly applies, rules to treat gaps between the installed Service Repository and the current state of installed Services on Service Platforms or equipment, identified by the Service Audit. <br/>These rules describe in which cases it is necessary to update the Service Repository, via the Service Repository Management, or to update the Service Platform itself (ex: Mobile Line and Voice Mail) through specific operations comparable to delivery ones. <br/>
