<<TMF_Function>>
Service Testing Management functions provides the necessary functionality to manage the end-to-end lifecycle of a test of a service.<br/>Including the management of scheduling, retrieval of appropriate inventory data, setting up the test configuration, acquisition and management of test resources, test results interpretation, reporting of test results and close down of the test of the service.<br/>
