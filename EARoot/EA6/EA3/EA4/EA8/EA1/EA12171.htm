Service Diagnostic and Test Analysis Function includes:<br/><ul>
<li>Management of success/failure result feedback of diagnostic and correction provided by technicians or the systems after a correction</li><li>Offline analysis of the success/failure data compared to the diagnostic and correction rules to provide modifications to improve them. This analysis is done using statistical diagnostic data and test results to evaluate their relevance and the needed updates.</li></ul><p/>
