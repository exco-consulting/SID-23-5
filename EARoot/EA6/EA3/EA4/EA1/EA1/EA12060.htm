Service Strategy Planning Function supports a high level planning of the strategy implementation. This high level plan contains for example the choice of technology with selected options (VHBB with FTTH or RFOG or both), the site management strategy, the selection of partners to provide contents. The strategy planning can take into account constraints (like legal constraints or the need of a validation by authorities) and notify other companies, local authorities or governments of the chosen strategy.<br/>
