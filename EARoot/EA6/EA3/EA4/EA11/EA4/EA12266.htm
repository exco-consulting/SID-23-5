Service Problem Diagnose Supporting Function supports the service trouble cause identification via diagnostic actions (Test, inventories and historics consultation, Workforce Intervention) and interpretation rules of their results.<br/>The identification of the service trouble cause can be made through:<br/><ul>
<li>Analysis of associated Service Trouble Ticket Information (first diagnostic)</li><li>Analysis of Service alarm events (fault of performance data)</li><li>Issuing service tests, including one time tests or a series of tests over a period of time</li><li>Analysis of previous service test results, service usage historic, QoS historic, service platform logs, equipment logs, anomaly historics, etc.</li></ul><p/>
