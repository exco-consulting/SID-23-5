Service Problem Types and Rules Design Function allows to manage the service problem typology describing all the types of known troubles related to a service type.<br/>It is also possible to define rules to manage Service Trouble Tickets and Service Trouble priorities, depending on SLA products subscribed (or assigned by regulatory authorities, to hospitals, strategic installations, etc.).<br/>
