<<TMF_Function>>
The Technical Solution Policy Management Function enables to define, and to check consistency of, potentially complex rules to automate the choice of the technical solution (CFS Spec) for a know-how (RFS Spec) or parts of the technical solution.<br/><br/>Criteria can be of various nature and combined:<br/><ul>
<li>geographical</li><li>depending on the chosen characteristics values of the know-how</li><li>depending on other products / know-hows of the customer installed or ordered</li><li>depending on the current state of Service Platforms, Network equipment (occupation rate, load balancing, etc )</li><li>depending on Supplier/Partner contracts (volume discounts, etc)</li><li>etc.</li></ul><p/>
