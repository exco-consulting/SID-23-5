<<TMF_Function>>
Resource Trouble Types and Rules Design Function allows to manage the resource problem typology describing all the types of known troubles related to a resource type.<br/>It is also possible to define rules to manage Resource Trouble Tickets and Resource Trouble priorities, depending on criteria such as:<br/><ul>
<li>Priority of the related Service Trouble Ticket(s) or Service Trouble(s) </li><li>Number of Product Trouble Tickets that can be expected, linked to the usage of the installed resource.</li></ul><p/>
