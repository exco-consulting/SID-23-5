<<TMF_Function>>
Resource Test Result Analysis Policy Configuration function provides the necessary functionality to manage how the Resource Test Strategy and Policy Management test results should be interpreted. Strategies can range from simple (e.g. - check the status of a router) to complex (e.g. - perform an end-to-end test on a circuit and sectionalize the problem).<br/>
