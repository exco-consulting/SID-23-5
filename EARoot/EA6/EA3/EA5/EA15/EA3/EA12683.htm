Identify Shortfalls in Physical Infrastructure Identifies shortfalls in infrastructure, such as building capacity, which may not be visible to central tactical planning users. This requires large amounts of data, potentially leading to a very expensive system solution. However, such systems are used locally for construction projects, so the system does not require a live view of the complete network. It is only necessary to administer a physical planning solution centrally to ensure data consistency; the data itself may be distributed to individual locations.<br/>
