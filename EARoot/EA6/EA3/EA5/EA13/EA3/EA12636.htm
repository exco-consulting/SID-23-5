<<TMF_Function>>
Because of the “Resource Task Item Decomposition” of an orchestration request the result may be several actions that needs to take place in a specific sequence. The “Resource Work Item Sequence Execution” function executes each individual item in sequence to fulfill, or roll back according to a pre-defined configuration, and reports the sequence carry through result.<br/>
