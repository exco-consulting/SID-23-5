Configurable Network Planning function is responsible for the planning and capacity management of configurable switch and wireless networks (OSI layers 4+). This includes actively managing firewalls, security, planning and capacity management of our national data centers responsible for SMS and MMS messaging. Additionally, this includes management of the VoIP platforms as well as trunk planning and forecasting for wireline and wireless networks. <br/>
