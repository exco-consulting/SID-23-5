Stock Item Requests may come from Customer orders or Stock Level replenishment rules.<br/>Stock Item Request Management function <br/>   - collects Stock Item Requests,<br/>   - determines actions (including Procurement or reallocation) to be triggered according to Stock Item Requests, Stock Item availability and Stock Level Rules. <br/>If the Stock Item is available where it is requested, it will assign Stock Item to the request. If it isn't available, it identifies either Stock Item Shipment (to the customer location or a warehouse) or a purchase request realized via Business Partner Order Management.<br/>
