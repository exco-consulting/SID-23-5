<<TMF_Function>>
Onboarded Product Partner Collaboration function defines the Point Of Contacts (POC) pertaining to the onboarded product offerings. The function supports the definition and management of the information about the POCs for both the sourcing partner and the receiving organization. The POCs may differ for different stages of the onboarded product e.g. onboarding, fulfillment, assurance, billing.<br/>
