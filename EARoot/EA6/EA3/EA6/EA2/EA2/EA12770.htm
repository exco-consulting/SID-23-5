Cost Avoidance Management Function measures Cost avoidance.<br/>Cost avoidance is much more difficult to define. Suggested definitions include: <br/>-avoidance is a cost reduction that does not lower the cost of products/ but rather minimizes or avoids entirely the negative impact to the bottom line that a price increase would have caused<br/><br/>-when there is an increase in output/capacity without increasing resource expenditure, in general, the cost avoidance savings are the amount that would have been spent to handle the increased volume/output<br/>-avoidances include process improvements that do not immediately reduce cost or assets but provide benefits through improved process efficiency, employee productivity, improved customer satisfaction, improved competitiveness, etc.; over time, cost avoidance often becomes cost savings.<br/><br/>
