Cost Reduction Management Function measures Cost reductions.<br/>Cost reductions, understood as tangible bottom line reductions, are easily defined as / characterized by: <br/>-year-on-year saving over the constant volume of purchased product/service<br/>-direct reduction of expense liked to changes in processes/technology/policies that directly reduces expenses,<br/>process improvements that result in real and measurable cost or asset reductions<br/>-examination of existing products or services, contractual agreements, or processes to determine potential changes that reduce cost<br/>-net reductions in prices paid for items procured when compared to prices in place for the prior 12 months or a change to lower cost alternatives.<br/><br/>
