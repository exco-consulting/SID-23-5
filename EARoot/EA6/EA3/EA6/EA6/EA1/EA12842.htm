<<TMF_Function>>
Purchase Requisition Management function manages the purchase requests and approvals and convert purchase approvals into standard purchase orders. It also manages requisitions for Network infrastructure maintenance and investment, only used for purchase of Network infrastructure. E.g.: Technical partner needs to purchase material for a planned or emergency infrastructure work. It may then be booked as an investment or maintenance expenditure.<br/>
