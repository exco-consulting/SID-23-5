Enterprise Email function supports organizational communication and information management. Its key responsibilities include:<br/>1. Managing Email Infrastructure: Ensuring the availability, scalability, and security of email servers and systems.<br/>2. User Account Management: Creating, maintaining, and deactivating email accounts for employees.<br/>3. Email Security: Implementing robust security measures to protect against phishing, malware, and unauthorized access.<br/>4. Email Compliance: Enforcing email policies, archiving, and retention to meet legal and regulatory requirements.<br/>5. Email Encryption: Securing sensitive data through encryption for confidentiality.<br/>6. Spam and Virus Protection: Filtering and blocking unwanted emails and threats.<br/>7. Email Client Support: Assisting users with email client configuration and troubleshooting.<br/>8. Disaster Recovery: Creating backup and recovery plans to ensure email continuity.<br/>9. Training and Education: Conducting training sessions to educate users on email best practices.<br/>10. Performance Monitoring: Constantly monitoring email system performance and optimizing for efficiency and reliability.<br/>
