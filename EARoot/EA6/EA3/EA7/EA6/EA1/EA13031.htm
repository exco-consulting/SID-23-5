Account Receivable Debit/Credit Matching function allocates/assigns receivables within customers account: Match credit and debit entries related to a same process. The most common example is matching payment(s) to corresponding invoices or invoice items. The matching does not affect the global balance but eases the clerical tasks.<br/>
