<<TMF_Function>>
Calculation Rules Retrieval function gives support for tariffication rules including discounting rules.<br/>It supports that discounting rules and guidelines are provided as to standard levels of discounts/promotions  that can be provided to the customer. Special discount arrangements can be obtained by following an escalation process. There is workflow functionality to help manage discount escalation.<br/>
