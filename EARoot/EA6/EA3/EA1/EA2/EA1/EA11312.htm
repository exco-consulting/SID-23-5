<<TMF_Function>>
The Market Information Gathering Function allows to collect information from external or internal sources, to obtain a better knowledge of the market and competitors.<br/>The information collected may concern, for example:<br/>- market, market shares, general trends, potentials,<br/>- customer and consumer expectations and motivations, new modes of consumption,<br/>- distribution, general trends, new distribution channels<br/>- competition, competitors, their market shares and strategy (positioning, target, etc.),<br/>- environment: politic, economic (cartography of the geo-merchandising zones), technologic, ecological, legislative<br/>- enterprise image & fame, etc.<br/>
