Sales Behaviors Identification Function defines behaviors models and strategy: <br/><ul>
<li>development of purchase behavior models that will be used for the marketing strategy development, in order to improve the distribution performance. These models will be used for the statistical tool implementation (customer profile type) and the creation of statistical data (dashboards).</li><li>a broad Sales Strategy to support the market strategy with a Sales model management defining the various stages of the sales pipeline, which can be based on the SPANCO method (Suspect qualification, Prospect qualification, Analyzing, Negotiating, Closing, Ordering).</li></ul><p/>
