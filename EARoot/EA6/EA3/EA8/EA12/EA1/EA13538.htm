<<TMF_Function>>
Business Operational Statistics Analyzing provides functions used for mathematical and statistical analysis of data. Information includes, but is not limited to:<br/><ul>
<li>on market: knowledge of the market shares by region with general trends, potentials in volume, in CA</li><li>on customer and consumer calculation of the customer average revenues according to the offer range and flow (acquisition, churn, migration)</li><li>on distribution: general trends</li><li>on competition: analysis of the market shares, positioning, target</li><li>on Company Performances: turnover evolution, profitability, financial means</li><li>market shares by region: penetration rates of the offers by region, number of eligible lines by region.</li></ul><p/>
