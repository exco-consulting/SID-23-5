The Content Presentation function supports delivering information effectively to audiences.- It strives to provide users with a consistent view of content regardless of the portal, device or access method used, and so that users do not need to know where content is authored. <br/>1. Structuring Content: Organizing information for clarity and accessibility, and according to recipient preferences.<br/>2. Visual Design: Creating a visually appealing layout and aesthetics, with consideration for platform-specific requirements, limitations, capabilities and norms.<br/>3. Consistent Branding: Ensuring content aligns with brand guidelines.<br/>4. Responsive Design: Adapting content for various devices and screens.<br/>5. Accessibility: Ensuring content is inclusive and user-friendly. Support any special needs for representing content by a particular device consistently (device independent) across platforms.
