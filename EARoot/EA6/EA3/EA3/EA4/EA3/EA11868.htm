Product Specification and Offering Change Auditing manages the implications of Product Specifications and Offerings changes to determine the consequences of any given change. Product Specifications and Offerings changes may impact other Product Specifications and / or Offerings according to relationships between them.<br/>The function logs Product Specifications changes and supports the analysis of relationships between Product Specifications. <br/>In addition, it tracks the history of changes in an easy and accessible manner.<br/>
