Product Specification and Offering Repository Management is able to create, modify and delete Product Specification and Offering.<br/>This includes the ability to manage the state of an entity during its lifecycle (e.g. planned, deployed, in operation, replaced by, locked).<br/>It includes Product Specifications and Offerings retrieval, integrity rules check and versioning management.<br/>It also provides Product Specification and Offering views adapted to the different roles.<br/>
