Product Storage provides the functionality necessary to store and make available the Products.<br/>This function allows:<br/>• to instantiate or update offers and products ordered by the customer, whatever their type (network product, bundle, device, …) or their marketing mode (rented, sold, …), with their configuration, their tariffs and discounts, and their status (initialized with a creation order)<br/>• to update Products status<br/>• to search and read Offer and Product installed base (subscribed offers, configuration of installed products, installed tariffs and discount, statuses, …).<br/>
