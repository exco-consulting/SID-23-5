The Product Performance Measurement Function enables Product functioning to be measured (in real-time or not).<br/><br/>It includes: <br/>• filtering, aggregation of the collected information<br/>• analysis of <br/>     • different types of information <br/>     • results of tests or historic of tests<br/>     • product usage<br/>     • traffic degradations. <br/>• enrich or calculate product quality and performance indicators (includes KQI, KPI…) <br/>     • Examples of KQI: time to repair warranty, availability percentage on a time slot, percentage of correct vocal quality communications.<br/>
