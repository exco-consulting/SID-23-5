<<TMF_Function>>
Product Performance Analysis Function comprises off-line analysis of product performance and forecasts concerning the product performance indicators, depending on rules and indicators defined through Product Supervision Strategy and Policy Management.<br/><br/>It calculates product quality and performance indicators (aggregated or not) and produces statistics and forecasts concerning the product quality and performance indicators, based on the following information: <br/>• indicators already calculated by the Product Quality and Performance Supervision function<br/>• aggregated product usages <br/>• historic of product test<br/>• product trouble ticket<br/><br/>The information produced can also be used to improve the rules and indicators definition of Product Supervision Strategy and Policy Management.<br/>
