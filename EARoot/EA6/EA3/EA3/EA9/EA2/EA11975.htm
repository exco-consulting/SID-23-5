<<TMF_Function>>
Customer Problem Status On-Request Reporting function provides the necessary functionality to report on the status of open / closed customer problems. Customer problems can include:<br/><ul>
<li>General questions on products purchased and being used by the customer</li><li>Problems with products already purchased and being used by the customer either due to lack of education or service/network problems</li><li>Problems with a material purchase from the service provider, even if they do not have an account with the said service provider</li><li>General inquiries, complaints, and commendations.</li></ul><p/>
