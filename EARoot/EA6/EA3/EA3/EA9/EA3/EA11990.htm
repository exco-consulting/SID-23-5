<<TMF_Function>>
The Product Trouble Ticket Prioritization Function prioritizes the Service Trouble Tickets for the processing of related Product Trouble Tickets.<br/>Orchestration can take into account:<br/>• constraints between installed products <br/>• any type of management rule based on information even external to trouble handling. For example: high priority for VIP customers.<br/>
