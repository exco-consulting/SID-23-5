Business Intelligence analytic functions used for mathematical and statistical analysis of data - calculating correlations, trends, and patterns using complex algorithms and modeling to support Operational Decision Support Systems. <br/>Information includes, but is not limited to  <br/>• on market; knowledge of the market shares by region with general trends, potentials in volume, in CA<br/>• on customer and consumer; expectations and motivations, new modes of consumption, calculation of the customer average revenues according to the offer range and flow (acquisition, churn, migration).<br/>• on distribution; general trends, new distribution channel<br/>• on competition; anticipate the competition strategy by analysis of the market shares and competition and evolution assumptions, positioning, target.<br/>• for environment; politic, economic (cartography of the geo-merchandising zones), technologic, ecological, legislative<br/>• on Company Performances; turnover evolution, profitability, financial means<br/>• for customers; enterprise image & fame, qualification of the customers (loyalty, satisfaction, contact mode, frequency, income, purchasing power, equipment…), purchase behavior<br/>• market shares by region; penetration rates of the offers by region, number of eligible lines by region<br/>
