Aggregate Party Items For Charging accumulates contributing items, which can be quantities, values (monetary or other) or both. Aggregation can occur over time or can be initiated to gather a “snapshot” of the items at a point in time.<br/><br/>The aggregated items may be used in Perform Party Rating or Apply Party Rate Level Discounts to determine the applicable price or discount and may further be used as a quantity in the calculation of a rate or discount.<br/>
