Acknowledge Product Offering Purchase Completion provides acknowledgement to the party making the product offering purchase that it is complete. The acknowledgement can be provided in a number of ways including email and text message.<br/><br/>It may also include acknowledgement of payment if one has been made as part of the purchase. The Party Payment Handling process is responsible for accepting payments.<br/>
