Select Outbound Purchase Party identifies the most appropriate party parties, to whom a  product offering purchase may be sent. The selection of the party is  based on the particular product, service and/or resource requirements and the feasibility of a party being able to meet the requirements.<br/>Select External Party identifies the most appropriate external party or parties amongst those with whom an appropriate agreement exists. Based on the particular product, service and/or resource requirements, select the most appropriate party from the range of parties with whom a agreement exists.<br/>
