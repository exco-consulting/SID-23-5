Make Product Offering Purchase Choice chooses a product offering to purchase or creates a customized product offering. A customized offering may be a bundle and is based on rules defined for creating the customized offering. A price for the customized offering may also be determined (not chosen).<br/><br/>The choice may also include one of the recommended product offerings provided.<br/>
