The objective of the Close Service Performance Degradation Report processes is to close a service performance degradation report when the service performance has been resolved.<br/>These processes monitor the status of all open service performance degradation reports, and recognize that a service performance degradation report is ready to be closed when the status is changed to cleared.<br/>
