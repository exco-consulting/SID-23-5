<<TMF_Process>>
Select Outbound Purchase Party identifies the most appropriate party or parties, to whom a  product offering purchase may be sent. The selection of the party is  based on the particular product, service and/or resource requirements and the feasibility of a party being able to meet the requirements.<br/>
