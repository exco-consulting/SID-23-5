<<TMF_Process>>
This process designs a Customer Experience journey for a specific Business Offering according to the desired outcome, the design uses a predefined Business Offering CE journey template. A customer experience journey encompasses a variety of customer interactions which can be described as a journey path from a starting trigger to an outcome. The process defines the set of interactions in the corresponding template for each customer journey. <br/>
