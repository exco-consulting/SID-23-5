<<TMF_Process>>
Customer Privacy Profile Management process is used to handle the life cycle of the customers privacy profile.<br/>This process is crucial to customer privacy management, because it helps the entities processing data to conform with Laws & Regulation and privacy by design principles. It is composed of:<br/><ul>
<li>Enforcement of the Customer Privacy Profile, meaning the application of the Privacy Rules for each purpose/retention, both internal and external, of a Customers personal identifiable information.</li><li>Import/export of the Privacy Profile for each element of personal identifiable information received or sent from or to another entity involved in the processing of customer data.</li><li>Modify a Privacy Profile, through Dashboards or consent.</li><li>Meet other requirements from regional Laws & Regulations, such as the demonstration of compliance, the Right of Erasure or Right to be forgotten.</li></ul><p/>
