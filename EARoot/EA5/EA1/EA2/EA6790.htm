<<TMF_Process>>
Provide notifications of any changes to the status of customer QoS performance degradation reports. These processes are responsible for managing notifications to other processes and to other parties, including customers, registered to receive notifications of any status changes.  Notification lists are managed and maintained by the Support Customer QoS/SLA Management processes.<br/>
