<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Customer Order Payment business activity directs and controls customer payments for orders received. It is responsible for ensuring that customer payments for orders are processed, and accounted for correctly. <br/><br/>This business activity ensures that payments are received, validated, correctly applied to the customers accounts, tracked for compliance, and where applicable the corresponding portion of the payment sent to upstream business partners.<br/>
