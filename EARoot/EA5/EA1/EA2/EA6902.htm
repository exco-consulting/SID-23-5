<<TMF_Process>>
The purpose of this process is to define the quality requirements of the customer experience in terms that can be objectively verified by decomposing the quality attributes into single, measurable attributes. Based on established goals, this process determines current achievement levels as a baseline for comparing future achievement levels.<br/>
