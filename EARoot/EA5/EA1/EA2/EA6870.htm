<<TMF_Process>>
This process receives data about Customer Experience journey interactions in a predefined Business Offering CE journey template, in order to record the customer experience for a specific Business Offering, for further analysis. This process feeds from information provided by the Customer Experience Collection & Correlation processes in the OR&S vertical.<br/>
