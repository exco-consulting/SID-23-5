Customer Strategy Management defines and oversees the distinctive value and experience the business will deliver to customers, along with offerings, channels, operating models, and capabilities needed to implement them. <br/><br/>Customer Strategy Management ensures understanding of customer needs and expectations, and developing strategies to meet those needs. In addition, it develops policies for engaging with existing and potential customers in support of market, sales, and operations strategies<br/>
