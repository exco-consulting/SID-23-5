Distribute Customer Problem Management Report includes tasks to create and send reports that contain the problem analysis, root cause, and solution provided management reports, with any specialized summaries of the efficiency and effectiveness of the overall Customer Problem Management process. These specialized summaries could be specific reports required by specific audiences.<br/>This process was renamed in 23.5 it was called Distribute Customer Problem Management Reports & Summaries<br/>
