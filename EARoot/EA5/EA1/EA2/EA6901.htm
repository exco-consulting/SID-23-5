Change Customer Order Policy business activity amends and remodels customer order guidelines and practices in support of the organization's customer management objectives. <br/><br/>Change Customer Order Policy ensures that changes to customer order policies are done with all concerned parties included in the decision to change a policy. This includes the impact on customer management process, impact on the customer, and all associated "Customer Order Policy" stakeholders.<br/>
