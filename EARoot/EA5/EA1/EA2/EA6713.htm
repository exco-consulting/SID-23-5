The objective of the Close Customer Bill Inquiry Report processes is to close a customer bill inquiry report when the bill inquiry/complaint has been resolved.<br/><br/>These processes monitor the status of all open customer bill inquiry reports, and recognize that a customer bill inquiry report is ready to be closed when the status is changed to cleared.<br/>
