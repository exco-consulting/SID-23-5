<<TMF_Process>>
The Customer Privacy Management Definition process manage the definition of the privacy management that applies to a particular category of customer.<br/>It is used to determine the legal expectations of entities that want to apply and use customer information (such as jurisdiction, location, regulation, and standards).<br/>
