Deliver Customer Engagement Capability processes are responsible for designing  the capabilities required to design and use internal views and Customer Journey templates to which particular customer engagements can be mapped and processed.<br/>Customer engagement has multiple interactions, multiple channels and multiple intents. The Deliver Customer Engagement Capability process creates capabilities to align specific engagement needs and also simplify Customer Journey views so that similar engagements can be unified into common process flows.<br/>was named Customer Engagement Capability Delivery<br/>
