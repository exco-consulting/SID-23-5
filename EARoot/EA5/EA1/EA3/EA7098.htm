<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Infrastructure Policy Lifecycle is performed in case a policy lifecycle process needs to become established to meet the challenges of a dynamic offering catalogue. This process would verify that the risks of carrying an offer were acceptable and initiate the development and maturation of any policy development activity to close any gaps.<br/><br/>This process is critical when managing virtual infrastructure such as Could resources or slices of virtual networks.<br/>
