<<TMF_Process>>
These processes ensure the co-coordinated delivery in line with the approved business case of all required product infrastructure capabilities for that business case across the enterprise. It is predominantly a program/project management function, with the detailed management of individual infrastructure component delivery managed through separate processes.<br/>
