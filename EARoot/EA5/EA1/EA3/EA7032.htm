Report Product Problem Solution business activity is in charge of documenting (written or verbal) the answer or corrective actions applied to resolve or mediate a product problem.<br/><br/>This business activity includes tasks that design product problem handling status reports, collecting information relevant to report product problem solutions,  development of the product problem solution report, review of the product problem reports fit-for-purpose, and distributing product problem reports.<br/>
