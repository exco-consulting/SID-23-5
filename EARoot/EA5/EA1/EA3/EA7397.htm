<<TMF_Process>>
Approve Product Order business activity is responsible for certifying that a product order, based on defined product order management business rules and policies, can be endorsed for further processing. <br/><br/>Approve Product Order business activity supports 'Manage Product Order Capture' to accept the product order according to business rules and policies. This business activity also supports the de-risk of product order placements or reduce impact on the integrity of the product ordering process.<br/><br/>This business activity includes actions that determine pre-purchase feasibility for the delivery of the products.<br/>
