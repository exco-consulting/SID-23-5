Mitigate Product Anomaly business activity is in charge of allaying or alleviate the impact of a product’s anomalous exception.<br/>Mitigate Product Anomaly identify and coordinate mitigatory actions required for product’s anomalous exceptions, including the initiation of remedial actions, and providing support to monitor and report on the mitigatory action.<br/>Mitigate Product Anomaly can ensure the effect of a detected anomalous exception is minimized or resolved through appropriate actions. The aim of this business activity is to reduce Product Problem Management occurrence.<br/>
