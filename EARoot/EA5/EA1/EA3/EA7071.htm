Analyze Product Performance business activity aggregates, provides research findings and product performance reports. Analyze Product Performance includes the collection and strategic evolution of data points (metrics, indicators etc.) related to the purchasing funnel, like how many times visitors view a product page, how many times a product is left abandoned in a cart, how many times a particular product gets to checkout, unique purchases, and more.<br/>
