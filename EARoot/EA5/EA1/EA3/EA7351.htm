Model Product Specification business activity is in charge of determining independent product specifications variables to include and exclude from asserting the product's specification relationships and connections.<br/><br/>The need for modelling product specifications often begins when a product manager seeks to define the relationship between independent product variables and the dependent product variable.<br/>This business activity is used by organizations to establish and manage the collection of standardized, pre-documented product specifications and their dependencies to guide Lifecycle Management and Operations.<br/>
