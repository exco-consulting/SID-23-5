Manage Infrastructure Fulfillment Orchestration Intent Lifecycle process ensures that the intents that intermittent orchestration processes provide are managed appropriately by the platform owner. This process needs to be continuous when infrastructure is virtual and the relationships between infrastructure elements are dynamic. (This process can be delivered within the design of persistent offers)<br/>
