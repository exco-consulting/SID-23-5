Configuration Management configures or creates a new version of a configuration for an entity, such as a product, service, or resource, as defined by  a configuration specification. This process also modifies a configuration and values for configuration parameters, and removes a configuration.<br/>The configuration of one entity may be required the configuration of other entities as defined by a selected configuration specification to use.  For example, the configuration of a product may require the configuration of a service and a physical resource, such as a device.<br/>
