Product Anomaly Lifecycle Management business process establishes and controls all activities that are involved in overseeing, directing, administering, controlling and organizing the definition, detection/prediction, mitigation and learnings related to Product Anomaly Management.<br/>Product Anomaly Lifecycle Management cover the lifecycle activities, including:<br/>Establishing “what is normal” for a active product (buy, sell, care) in order to inform “deviations” or aberrations.<br/>Orchestrating Product Anomaly Management activities to meet customer and business partner needs.<br/>Managing the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills from past and present product anomalies.<br/>Managing actions that make the best or effective use of Product Management Closed Loops.<br/>Monitoring feedback to and from Product Management to support Product Anomaly Management activities.<br/>Reporting on Product Anomaly Management Closed Loops.<br/>
