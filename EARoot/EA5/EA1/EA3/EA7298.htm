The purpose of the Support Product Balance Management process is to make sure that there is capability (for example, information, systems and resources) so that the Support Product Balance Management process can operate effectively. For example policy defined to mange balance (e.g. minimum allowable balance limit, balance expiration dates), account information needed to operating the balance.<br/>These processes undertake trend analysis on balance management.<br/>
