Manage Product Offering Development ensures the co-coordinated delivery in line with the approved business case of all required product offering capabilities for that business case across the enterprise. These processes may use project management disciplines when a project is created to develop a new product offering to deliver the necessary capabilities, including process development, specific systems & network infrastructure developments, specific channel developments, specific operational procedures, and so forth. required to support the new product offering. <br/>Note that delivery of a product offering within the context of existing commercial agreements is managed through the Party Offering Development & Retirement process. If new partners are required, the Party Agreement Management process is used to deliver the necessary commercial agreements.<br/>Note that the management of major new or enhanced infrastructure development to support Product & Offer Development is managed within the Product & Offer Capability Delivery process<br/>
