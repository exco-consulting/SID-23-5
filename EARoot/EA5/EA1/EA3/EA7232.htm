Exit Product Offering from Market removes a product specification, either by deleting it or indicating it is no longer valid. Notifications are sent to the Product Specification Development & Retirement process so it can determine if the related specifications should also be removed. Additionally a notification may be sent to the Party Offering Development & Retirement process so that it can determine if any on-boarded product offerings should also be removed.<br/>
