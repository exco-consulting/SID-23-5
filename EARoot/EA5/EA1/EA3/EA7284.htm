Develop Algorithmic Product Offering Pricing Matrix Specification defines (creates, modifies, and removes) the matrix and its rows and columns and other dimensions of a multi-dimensional matrix. The rows and columns represent the criteria evaluated to determine the charge/allowance. They represent such things as types of product features/properties, types of product offering prices, algorithmic specific criteria such as type of customer, and so forth.<br/><br/>Note that properties/features are also known as characteristic specifications.<br/>
