Customer hierarchies are commonly used for corporate customers, family plans or other type of affinity groups. This process manages the assignment relationships among subscribers, e.g. sharing, inheriting or restricting balances, price plans and discounts. Thereby assuring that a charge is added to or subtracted from the correct account balance.<br/>
