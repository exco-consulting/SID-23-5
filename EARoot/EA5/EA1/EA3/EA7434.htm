<<TMF_Process>>
Deliver Product Order business activity is responsible for the actual delivery activity of the product order fulfilled by the business to the consignee. It includes ensuring the product order can be activated/is activated, tracking the product order delivery and notifying product order delivery. <br/><br/>Deliver Product Order ensures that the chosen channel and delivery plan based on business rules and/or requirement of the consignee are followed in the actual delivery to the consignee.<br/>
