Manage Product Anomaly Case History business activity records all past information on Product Anomaly events, Product Anomaly Management activities and data underlying, and representing Product Anomaly cases.<br/>Manage Product Anomaly Case History includes the maintenance of past and present records, all artifacts, knowledge-base (e.g. the specifications, profiles, exception patterns, action patterns etc.) and versioned information.<br/>This business activity supports the organization to maintain skills and learning repository to identify and predict Anomalous Exception patterns and mitigatory actions.<br/>
