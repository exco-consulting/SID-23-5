Activate Product Order Item business activity is responsible for making a product order item active. <br/><br/>Activate Product Order is available when an order is either ready to be shipped or provisioned. This business activity may require the consignee to initiate the request for activation through a request/response interaction, or by getting the product provision at the time of shipping.<br/>
