Develop Product Offering Price Action develops (creates, updates, and removes) a product offering price action that represents the outcome of the successful evaluation of one or more policy conditions. <br/><br/>An action is part of a policy rule that is made up of a conditions and actions.  Conditions and actions are defined by policy statements.  For example a statement for an action may be "get two software packages from a choice of five for free".<br/>
