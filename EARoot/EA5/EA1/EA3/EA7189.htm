<<TMF_Process>>
Analyze Product Capacity business activity establishes and models insights, patterns and changes to product capacity, by assessing potential capacity and the actual capacity a product currently achieves.<br/><br/>This business activity covers collection of product-related data (usage data, performance data, operational data etc.) to support Product Managers to identify patterns and insights relating to a product.<br/><br/>Analyze Product Capacity support Product Capacity Management by providing relevant and timely insights, including available inventory holding capacity, products "time and material" capacity, etc. and how capacity utilization (allocated, in-use, unused) can be optimized to increase business value.<br/>
