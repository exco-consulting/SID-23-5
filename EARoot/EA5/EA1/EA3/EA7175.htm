Define Product Specification develops the product specification, which also includes grouping/bundling of specifications and versioning of the specifications. Groups/bundles may include specifications on-boarded from other parties. The groups/bundles typically contain product specifications that are always included together and not separately priced.<br/><br/>When necessary version information, such as version number, version date, and a description of the version, are created.<br/>
