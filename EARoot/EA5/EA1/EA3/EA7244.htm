<<TMF_Process>>
The Capture Product Capability Shortfalls processes identify specific or imminent product capacity, product performance and/or product operational support shortfalls. These processes take information available from the Customer Relationship Management processes to establish detailed views of anticipated product capacity and performance shortfalls and support process issues.<br/>
