Manage Product Anomaly Optimization business activity is in charge of actions that make the best and most effective use of Product Anomaly Management activities to improve Product Anomaly Management.<br/>Manage Product Anomaly Optimization business activity can manage the business logic and business rules associated with Product Anomaly Detection, Product Anomaly Assessment, Product Anomaly Forecasting and Prediction, Product Anomaly Mitigation, as well as underlying Product Knowledge Management.<br/>This business activity also includes fine-tuning of business logic, operations logic, business and operations rules that improve efficiency of Anomaly Management as a whole.<br/>
