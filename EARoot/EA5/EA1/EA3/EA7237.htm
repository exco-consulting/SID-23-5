<<TMF_Process>>
Monitor Marketing Campaign Effectiveness establishes metrics, monitors metrics, and analyzes metrics to gauge the effectiveness of a marketing campaign or campaigns. Based on analysis these processes feedback suggested changes to adapt the campaign or campaigns to become more effective now or in the future.<br/><br/>Note that this process' sub-processes reuse a generalized set of metric processes to develop, gather, and analyze (to some degree) marketing campaign metrics.<br/>
