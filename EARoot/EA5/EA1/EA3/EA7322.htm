<<TMF_Process>>
The purpose of the Report Product Usage Record processes is to generate reports on Product Usage records based on requests from other processes.<br/>These processes produce reports that may identify abnormalities, which may be caused by fraudulent activity or related to customer complaints.<br/>Investigation of problems related to these product usage records is also part of this process.<br/><br/>These processes also support other processes such as customer review of product usages (pre-billing and post-billing).<br/>
