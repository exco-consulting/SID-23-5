Develop Product Offering Exit Strategies develops infrastructure transition and/or replacement strategies, and manage the operational aspects of the exit process. A business proposal identifying the competitive threats, risks and costs may be required as part of developing the exit strategy. It includes any interaction with cross-enterprise co-ordination and management functions to ensure that the needs of all stakeholders are identified and managed.<br/>
