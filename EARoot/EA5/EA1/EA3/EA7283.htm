Develop Algorithmic Product Offering Pricing Matrix Cells defines the content of the product offering pricing matrix cells. <br/><br/>The cells represent the values for the product features/properties, such as excess data usage rates, product offering prices that will be charged/allowed, and values for algorithmic evaluation such as student, normal, or very important person (VIP) types of customers.<br/><br/>Note that features/properties are also known as characteristic specification values.<br/>
