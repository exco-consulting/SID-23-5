<<TMF_Process>>
Develop Detailed Product Offering develops and documents the detailed product offering-related commercial and performance specifications. These processes develop and document the features specific to an offering as defined by the associated product specification. The processes ensure that all detailed offerings are produced and appropriately documented. Additionally the processes ensure that the documentation is captured in an appropriate enterprise repository.<br/><br/>Note: To minimize the number of sub-processes that update and remove various aspects of a product offering, such as features and associated sales channels and locations, it is assumed that the "develop" processes also perform updates and removals.<br/>
