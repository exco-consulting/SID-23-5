<<TMF_Process>>
processes manage the end-to-end execution of a test or test scenario for resources not specific to a customer. Tests can be manual or automated.<br/>Resource Test Management processes rely on Product and Service Manage Resource Test process due to dependencies between product tests, service tests and resource tests.<br/>Resource Test Management includes:<br/>- Identification of resource tests needed according to fulfillment and assurance issues<br/>- triggering of resource tests in manual or automated mode<br/>- Execution of resource tests<br/>- Verification of test authorization (role and context) and quota management<br/>- Identification and prioritization of tests <br/>- Setting up the test context and configuration<br/>- Triggering appropriate product and service tests<br/>- Tests can be on-demand or planned according to specific needs<br/>- Enrichment with product and service tests results based on applicable resource test rules <br/>- Reporting of test results<br/>
