<<TMF_Process>>
Resource Order Management business process directs and controls ordering, scheduling, and allocation of resources (such as materials, equipment, and personnel) within the business. <br/><br/>Resource Order Management includes managing the capture of resource orders, scheduling works to support the resource order, managing the fullfillment of resource orders, picking/packing, shipping, tracking and closing orders.<br/>
