<<TMF_Process>>
Analyze new resource requirements  processes focus on research and analyze customer, technology, competitor, internal resource utilization trends and marketing information to identify new resource requirements and industry resource capabilities and availability. In some cases these processes may leverage information through the use of advanced analytics tools or AI for  fast, accurate and proactive forecasting.<br/>
