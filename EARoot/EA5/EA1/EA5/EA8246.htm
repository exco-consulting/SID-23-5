Pick Resource Order Items gathers the appropriate item/s from inventory to fulfill the resource order. <br/><br/>Pick Resource Order Items includes activities to locate the items from the right inventory/inventories, managing the physical of virtual picking of the items in readiness for onward packaging. This activity coordinates and is supported by warehousing, quality control, logistics and accurate and timely fulfillment of the resource order.<br/>
