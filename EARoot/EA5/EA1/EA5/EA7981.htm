<<TMF_Process>>
This process is responsible for continuously monitoring the status of Resource Performance Degradation Reports and managing notifications to processes  in the Resource-Ops and other layers, and to other parties registered to receive notifications of any status changes.  Notification lists are managed and maintained by the Enable Resource Performance Management processes.<br/>
