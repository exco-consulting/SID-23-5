Resource Test Development & Retirement is in charge of the Resource Test catalogue.<br/>A type of Resource Test aims at measuring proper functioning and capacities of a Resource.<br/>Resource Test Development & Retirement includes:<br/>   - Specifying in detail each Resource Test according to the different context. It includes specifying:<br/>        - the roles authorized to use the Test and quotas for each type of role<br/>       - the method to conduct the Test<br/>       - the rules that define the strategies for conducting the test (including  the test plan)<br/>       - the thresholds and related actions<br/>       - the report of test results with rules for enrichment of Resource Tests results according to role asking for it<br/>   - Specifying test scenarios defining sequence of Tests with rules about context and planning to trigger it. It includes roles allowed for asking test scenario and corresponding quotas.<br/>
