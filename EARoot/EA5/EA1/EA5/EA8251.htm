Manage Resource Order Fulfillment Profile business activity directs and controls the fulfillment information (e.g. set of pre-defined rules and procedures) that dictate how resource orders are processed. <br/><br/>Manage Resource Order Fulfillment Profile business activity includes tasks that detail any preferred shipping methods, handling instructions, and special requirements or considerations that must be taken into account when fulfilling a particular type of resource order. This business activity helps to streamline resource order fulfillment processing, helping to ensure that resource orders are processed and delivered with minimal or no risk.<br/>
