<<TMF_Process>>
This process is responsible for continuously monitoring the status of resource trouble reports and managing notifications to processes and other parties registered to receive notifications of any status changes, for example, Resource Performance Management and Service Quality Management.  Notification lists are managed and maintained by the Support Resource Trouble Management processes.<br/>
