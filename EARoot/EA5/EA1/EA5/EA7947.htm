<<TMF_Process>>
The Manage Number Portability Requests process ensures that the portability activities are assigned and managed efficiently and effectively.<br/>Responsibilities of these processes include, but are not limited to:<br/>•       Modifying or canceling open number portability requests<br/>•       Modifying the number portability request status<br/>•       Scheduling number portability request<br/>•       Adding additional information to number portability request<br/>•       Reporting the completion status of number portability request<br/>•       Managing communication flow with Number Portability Clearinghouse<br/>
