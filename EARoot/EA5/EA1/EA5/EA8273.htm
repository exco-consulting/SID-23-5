Assign Resource Work Order business activity dispatches work order tasks to responsible parties (resources) to execute the work order tasks. <br/><br/>Assign Resource Work Order business activity ensures that work order is completed with efficient allocation and use of supporting resources. Underlying activities includes identifying all supporting resources (e.g. agents, task crew, field agents etc. ) for the work order, determining their availability and suitability, and allocating them to the work order task according to the schedule and priorities established.<br/>
