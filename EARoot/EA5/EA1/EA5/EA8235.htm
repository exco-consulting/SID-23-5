Plan Resource Work Order business activity is responsible for planning and managing the scheduling of appointments for the work order and associated tasks that need to be done to fulfill the resource work order. <br/><br/>Plan Resource Work Order business activity includes determining the availability of the necessary resources, such as materials, equipment, and personnel, and creating a fulfillment schedule for the work to be completed on time, and that there are any disruptions are effectively managed to support the resource work order.<br/>
