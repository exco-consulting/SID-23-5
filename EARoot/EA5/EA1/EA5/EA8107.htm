<<TMF_Process>>
The objective of the Close Service Trouble Report processes is to close a service trouble report when the service problem has been resolved. <br/>These processes monitor the status of all open service trouble reports, and  recognize that a service trouble report is ready to be closed when the status is changed to cleared.<br/>
