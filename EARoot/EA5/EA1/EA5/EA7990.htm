<<TMF_Process>>
Resource Test Strategy develops the strategies of the enterprise for Resource Test.<br/>This process is in charge of identifying types of Resource Test to be conducted according to different context (i.e. business activities) for types of Resources.<br/>The different context for Resource Test are:<br/>   - Resource Development & Retirement to qualify the capacity to deliver Resources before validating a new ResourceSpecification<br/>   - Resource Provisioning to test the Resource before closing the ResourceOrderItem<br/>   - Resource Performance Management to test Resource Quality<br/>   - Resource Trouble Management to test Resource functioning<br/>   - Resource Test Management to conduct tests not specific to a customer’s product<br/>
