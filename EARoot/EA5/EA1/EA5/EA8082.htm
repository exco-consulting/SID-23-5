<<TMF_Process>>
The Manage Number Portability process is responsible for receiving, managing and tracking number portability requests issued by customer orders or a third CSP.  The number portability process allows the execution of different types of portability such as Port In, Port Out, Port Over and Retirement from Number Portability. <br/>  <br/>Responsibilities of these processes include, but are not limited to:<br/>• Issuing and reception of number portability requests<br/>• Modifying number portability request status<br/>• Validating number portability requests<br/>• Modifying or canceling open number portability requests<br/>• Undertaking necessary tracking of the execution process<br/>• Adding additional information to number portability request<br/>• Reporting the completion status of number portability request<br/>• Receiving portability requests from a third CSP<br/>• Scheduling number portability request<br/>• Managing the communication flow with Number Portability Clearinghouse <br/>• Implementing business rules<br/>• Implementing regulatory policies <br/>• Determining the number portability feasibility<br/>
