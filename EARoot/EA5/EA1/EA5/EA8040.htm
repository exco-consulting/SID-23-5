Plan Resource Capacity business activity is in charge of determining the production capacity needed to meet changing demand for resources over a period of time, and implements the plan.<br/>Plan Resource Capacity  ensures Resource Management aligns capacity planning strategies with business goals; while ensuring that there are sufficient resources or supporting resource capabilities to support planned and forecasted business objectives; identifying gaps in capacity allocation early; determining resource capacity needs according to demand, determining constraints and usage patterns over time; forecasting future capacity needs; and communicating resource capacity requirements in time.<br/><br/>In the context of capacity planning, consideration is given to all input requirements, conversion processes and outputs.<br/>
