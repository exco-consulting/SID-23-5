<<TMF_Process>>
The purpose of the Track & Manage Number Portability Requests is to ensure that the portability activities are assigned, managed and tracked efficiently in order to meet the business rules and regulatory policies. <br/>Responsibilities of these processes include, but are not limited to:<br/>•     Modifying the number portability request status<br/>•     Validating number portability request<br/>•     Modifying or canceling open number portability request<br/>•     Undertaking necessary tracking of the execution progress<br/>•     Adding additional information to number portability request<br/>•     Reporting the completion status of number portability request<br/>•     Generating number portability reports<br/>•     Implementing business rules <br/>•     Implementing regulatory policies<br/>
