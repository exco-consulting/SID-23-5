Resource Anomaly Lifecycle Management business process establishes and controls all activities that are involved in overseeing, directing, administering, controlling and organizing the definition, detection/prediction, mitigation and learnings related to Resource Anomaly Management.<br/><br/>Resource Anomaly Lifecycle Management cover the lifecycle activities, including:<br/>Establishing “what is normal” for Resource in order to inform “deviations” or aberrations.<br/>Orchestrating Resource Anomaly Management activities to meet business needs.<br/>Managing the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills from past and present Resource anomalies.<br/>Managing actions that make the best or effective use of Resource Management Closed Loops.<br/>Monitoring feedback to and from Resource Management to support Resource Anomaly Management activities.<br/>Reporting on Resource Anomaly Management Closed Loops.<br/>
