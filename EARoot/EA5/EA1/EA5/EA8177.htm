The objective of "Decide Self Healing Option" process is a responsibility to decide how recover the failure based on a self healing policy ( e.g. total number of times of violating a certain threshold, emergency level of a detected alarm or service level agreement that depends on each service provider). For example, the patterns of recover are divided into following patterns:<br/> - Physical network function self healing<br/> - Virtual network self function healing<br/> - Network service self healing<br/>
