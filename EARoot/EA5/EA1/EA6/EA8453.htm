Identify Revenue Share Item determines the items that will be used to calculate a revenue share for a product offering and aggregates them if called for by the associated revenue sharing model. The items may be product prices paid to acquire an offering if a bill/invoice charge is not created for it or applied billing rates, which could be a charge, discount, usage charge, and so forth.<br/>
