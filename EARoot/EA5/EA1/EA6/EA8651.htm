Identify Security or Privacy Interests of Partner's Consumers informs any strategy or intents adopted or executed by the enterprise.<br/>Note:<br/>An enterprise needs to continuously validate and correct its understanding of any participating partner's consumer's interests.  Most markets are subject to shifting concerns, and changes in how partner's consumers view their exposures to those concerns.  <br/><br/>This process looks at the rights and privacy needs of the organization's partners consumers, that would still require a legal or ethical obligation.<br/>
