Accept Party Payment accepts a payment made by a party that references a party or an account in the name of a party. The payment may reference a purchase/bill/charge.<br/><br/>The payment method includes credit/debit cards, checks, cash, intermediaries, such as PayPal or a bank, who may make a payment on behalf of a party. This process also ensures that if a purchase/bill/charge or any other form of request for payment is indicated that the application specified is correct.<br/>
