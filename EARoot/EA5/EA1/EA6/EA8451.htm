Initiate Business Partner Revenue Share Determination identifies that a revenue share needs to be calculated for a Business Partner with whom revenue is shared.  This can be done by assessing Business Partner revenue sharing agreements and the terms/conditions that state the time period for which revenue shares are determined for the parties involved in the agreement.<br/>
