Close Business Partner Problem closes a Business Partner problem when a problem has been resolved. It monitors the status of all open Business Partner problems and recognizes that a Business Partner problem is ready to be closed when the status is changed to resolved.<br/><br/>This process provides this information to Provide Business Partner Problem Change Notification which notifies applicable parties that a problem has been closed.<br/>
