Remove Party Demographic removes a Party demographic, either by deleting it or indicating it is no longer valid.  For example, a Party privacy agreement may specify that a certain fact about a part be removed via deletion after an agreed upon period of time; this is typically referred to "as the right to be forgotten".<br/>
