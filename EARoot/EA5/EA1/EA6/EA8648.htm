<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Business Partner Privacy Profile process is used to handle the life cycle of the business partners privacy profile.<br/>This process is crucial to business partner privacy management, because it helps the partnering entities processing data to conform with Laws & Regulation and 'privacy by design' principles.<br/>It is composed of:<br/>Enforcement of the Business Partner Privacy Profile, meaning the application of the Privacy Rules for each purpose/retention, both internal and external, of all personal identifiable information.<br/>Import/export of the Privacy Profile for each element of personal identifiable information received or sent from or to another partner or party involved in the processing of personal identifiable information.<br/>Modify a Privacy Profile, through dashboards or consent<br/>Meet other requirements from regional Laws & Regulations, such as the demonstration of compliance, the 'right of erasure' or 'right to be forgotten'.<br/>
