Prepare Business Partner Payment Plan defines basic Business Partner payment plan information, such as the amount and frequency of payments, as well as one or more payment methods that may be used to make payments.    <br/>Other aspects of the payment plan, such as terms and involved product offerings are defined by the Business Partner Agreement Management process which is reused to create agreements with parties.<br/>
