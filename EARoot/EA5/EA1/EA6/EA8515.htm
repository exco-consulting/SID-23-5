<<TMF_Process>>
Reconcile Business Partner Revenue Share identifies and resolves discrepancies and identifies revenue share adjustments. This is done by comparing the revenue determined by an enterprise that is to be shared with another Business Partner with the information provided by the Business Partner.<br/><br/>Adjustments may be made to the revenue share determined by an enterprise and/or to the information provided by another Business Partner.<br/>
