De-activate Off-boarded Preconfigured Services deactivates any services have been pre-configured for the associated party's product specification.  This is accomplished by notifying the  Service Configuration & Activation to de-activate them. If the deactivated services are the final ones associated with a service specification the Manage Service Exit process is notified for possible removal of the service specification.<br/>
