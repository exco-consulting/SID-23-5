<<TMF_Process>>
Monitor & Control Business Partner  Performance processes control the performance measurement activities, collect performance data on a specified  product, analyze this against the relevant SLA for the Business Partner and report performance data and any SLA violations to other processes. These processes also carry out impact analysis on any  SLA violations and initiate corrective actions.<br/>
