Receive Business Partner Bill Inquiry creates a Business Partner bill inquiry. A Business Partner bill inquiry may be created as a result of a specific Business Partner initiated bill inquiry or complaint. It may also request and receive additional information from a Business Partner if needed to further process the inquiry.<br/>
