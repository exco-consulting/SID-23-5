Specify Party Agreement Terms and Conditions for the agreement. These are<br/>aspects of an agreement not formally specified elsewhere in the agreement and that cannot be captured elsewhere in a formal notation, or automatically monitored and require a more human level of management.  Examples include definition of key words and phrases or user rights and responsibilities.<br/><br/>Terms and conditions may be associated with an entire agreement or with one or more items on an agreement.<br/>
