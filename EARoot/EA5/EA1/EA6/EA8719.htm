<<TMF_Process>>
The purpose of this process is to notify the Business Partner when events related to existing interactions or to significant Business Partner experience happen. Some notifications can be sent immediately using interactive media( such as SMS, Push to applications, etc.) and other notifications can be sent later using asynchronous  media such as mail.<br/>
