Define Payment Method Type define a specification that represents a method by which a payment may be made by a Business Partner. It also defines attributes other than base attributes/properties that are specific to a certain payment method. For example, information used to verify the identity of a Business Partner making a payment. These other properties are supported by a generalized set of processes that support the definition of these properties, called characteristics.<br/>
