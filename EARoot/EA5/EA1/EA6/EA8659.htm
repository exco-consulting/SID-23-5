<<TMF_Process>>
Performs identification of the necessary data formats to be sent externally.  Increasingly transactions with external parties (e.g. Parties using RosettaNet or Web Services standards) will need to conform to message and data formats defined by third parties or third Business Partner organizations. The actual agreement between the parties to use specific interaction standards is part of the Support Business Partner Interface Management and Support Selling L3s.<br/>
