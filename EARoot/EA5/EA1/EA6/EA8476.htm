Off-Board Business Partner Product Offering off-boards a Business Partner's product offering. This can occur when an agreement or an item on an agreement associated with a product offering is terminated.<br/><br/>When the final product offering related to a product specification is off-boarded the product specification may also be off-boarded by the Off-Board Business Partner Product Specification process.<br/><br/>Note that this process will interact with the Manage Product Offering Exit process which is part of Product Offering Development & Retirement.<br/>
