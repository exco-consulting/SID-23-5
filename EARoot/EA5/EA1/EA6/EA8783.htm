Resolve Business Partner Bill Inquiry resolves a Business Partner bill inquiry. It adjusts a Business Partner bill/invoice if an adjustment is warranted and approved and makes the results of the assessment and investigation available to other processes. It records how the inquiry was resolved. It also updates the status of the Business Partner bill inquiry to resolved and informs the Close Business Partner Bill Inquiry process that a Business Partner bill inquiry/complaint issues have been resolved.<br/>
