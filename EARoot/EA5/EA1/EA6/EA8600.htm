Welcome New Party provides an initial welcome to the Party with whom a relationship has started. The welcome may be communicated in a number of ways, such as a phone call, email, or visit to a Party.<br/><br/>A visit may be used if a Party is considered to be one in a top tier, such as a customer or partner who will contribute a significant amount of revenue to an enterprise. Scheduling, conducting, next steps, and evaluating a meeting are the responsibility of a appointment/calendaring process that would reside in Enterprise domain/Common Enterprise Processes; it has yet to be developed.<br/><br/>The communication would include:<br/>-Executive & team introduction<br/>-Access/explanation to the further on-boarding process<br/>-Access to partner portal<br/><br/>The communication should provide the right level of detail to get the new Party started without being overwhelming.<br/>
