<<TMF_Process>>
Collect Party Data collects data about a Party and/or a Party playing a role.  Data includes basic Party data, identification data, contact data, and additional attributes. This process also enables  modification and removal of Party data and Party role data and establishes the types of roles a Party can play.<br/>
