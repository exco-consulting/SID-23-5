<<TMF_Process>>
Begin Party Agreement Preparation prepares common agreement attributes, such as the agreement number, its date, period, statement of intent,  locations to which the agreement applies, and so forth.<br/><br/>For Party specific agreements it also specifies the parties engaged in the agreement, such as an enterprise and a partner, the roles involved in the agreement, such as various approvers or reviewers, as well as if the role is involved in authorizing the agreement.<br/><br/>For template agreements it specifies the types of parties that will be engaged in an agreement based on the template as well as their role, including involvement in authorization.<br/><br/>Relationships with other agreements may also be defined.<br/>
