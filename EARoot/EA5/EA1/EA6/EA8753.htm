Remove Business Partner Revenue Sharing Model removes a Business Partner revenue sharing model when it is no longer of interest to an enterprise. This may take place as part of removing a Business Partner revenue sharing agreement. The removal may be physical or logical by changing the validity period or the status of the model.<br/>
