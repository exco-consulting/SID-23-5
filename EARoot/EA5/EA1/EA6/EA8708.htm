Manage Business Partner Account Balance Policy manages a rule that describes how Business Partner account balance affecting  events are to be handled.      <br/><br/>This is done  by comparing the value of each event against criteria, such as the following:  <br/>- a minimum allowable balance limit. For example, a balance must remain above zero  <br/>- balance expiration dates  <br/>- balance thresholds actions and notifications  <br/>- roll-over & cyclic policies.<br/>
