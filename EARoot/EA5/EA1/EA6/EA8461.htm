Isolate Business Partner Problem identifies the root cause of a Business Partner problem. It verifies whether the product is being used correctly, performs diagnostics based on information provided by a Business Partner to determine whether the root cause of the Business Partner problem is linked to the underlying services/resources. It also updates a Business Partner problem, as required during the assessment, and when the root cause has been identified.<br/>
