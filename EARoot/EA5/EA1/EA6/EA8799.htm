<<TMF_Process>>
Initiate Business Partner Order processes is responsible for generating a correctly formatted and specified Business Partner order and issuing this to the selected Business Partner. A request for an order is passed to the from the Track & Manage processes in the Customer, Service, and Resource domains. The Initiate Business Partner Order contains the originating request identifier to allow for appropriate linking to the processes which originally caused the Business Partner order to be initiated.<br/>
