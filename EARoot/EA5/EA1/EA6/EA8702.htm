Record Business Partner Bill Inquiry Resolution resolves a Business Partner bill inquiry and records how the inquiry was resolved. Resolution may have been reached via the Make Business Partner Bill/Invoice Adjustment process, a Business Partner accepting the content of a bill/invoice which resulted in a Business Partner bill inquiry, or some other reason.<br/>
