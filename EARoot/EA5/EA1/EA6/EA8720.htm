<<TMF_Process>>
The purpose of this process is to ensure that Business Partner Interactions are managed and tracked efficiently across all interaction channels in order to meet the agreed SLA with the Business Partner. Responsibilities of these process include but are not limited to:<br/>· Ensure interactions are completed and closed in a timely manner<br/>· Monitor the interaction and Notify the Business Partner when SLA or deadlines can not be met<br/>· Track Business Partner responses when these are required<br/>· Measure KPIs in order to improve the Interactions efficiency<br/>
