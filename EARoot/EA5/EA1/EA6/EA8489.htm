Assign Privileges to Parties is responsible to set the party's level of privileges.<br/>Note:<br/> A parties' ability to interact with an organization is bounded by the privileges the organization assigns to the party.  While privileges are defined by strategy, policy and intent, they have a profound impact on the parties' experience of the organization, and thus need to be assigned with care.  Privileges are typically assigned to a parties identity, and are accessed from the context of that identity.<br/>
