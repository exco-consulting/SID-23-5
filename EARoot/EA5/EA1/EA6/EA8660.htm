<<TMF_Process>>
The purpose of the Mediate Business Partner Interactions is to ensure that transaction message structure and interactions conform to agreed or externally defined or agreed standards used by the enterprise and its Parties. Increasingly transactions with external parties (e.g. Parties using RosettaNet or Web Services standards) will need to conform to message and data formats defined by third parties or third Business Partner organizations.<br/>
