Develop Party Relationship Success Business Plan creates an initial engagement plan which includes rules of engagement, initial plans for training, describes on-boarding processes in more detail such as profile, demographic. For partners it further describes Party or enterprise product offering on-boarding and agrees to marketing activities. It also defines how an enterprise and a Party will both measure success.<br/>
