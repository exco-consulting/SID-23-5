Resolve Business Partner Revenue Share Discrepancy contacts a Business Partner regarding a discrepancy, works with the Business Partner to identify the cause of the discrepancy, and determines how to resolve the discrepancy.<br/><br/>Because different forms of information can be provided by a Business Partner other processes may be involved. For example, if an bill/invoice is provided by a Business Partner then the Business Partner Bill Inquiry Handling process may be invoked; if a payment is provided then the Business Partner Payment Handling process may be invoked; if a revenue share notification is provided then the Identify Business Partner Revenue Share Adjustment process may be invoked.<br/>
