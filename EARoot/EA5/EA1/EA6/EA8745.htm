Collect Basic Party Data collects basic data about a Party, such as name, billing and bank accounts and account contacts (such as email and mobile number of the Party as well as other parties and their email and mobile number), credit profiles, and language ability. It also establishes relationships among Party accounts and Party roles.<br/><br/>It also establishes a type of role a Party can play as well as categorizations of roles.<br/>
