Assess Business Partner Problem assesses a Business Partner problem to determine the nature of a problem and to isolate a problem. <br/><br/>The responsibilities of these processes include, but are not limited to:<br/>- Involving external parties if necessary to assess a Business Partner problem<br/>- Verifying whether the information supplied by a Business Partner is correct<br/>- Categorizing a Business Partner problem<br/>- Updating the Business Partner problem as required during the assessment and when the root cause has been identified.<br/>
