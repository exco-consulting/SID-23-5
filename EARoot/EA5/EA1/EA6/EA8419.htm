Party Relationship Development & Retirement manages the lifecycles of parties with whom the enterprise has a relationship. Relationship with new parties may be required to broaden the services an enterprise offers, to improve performance, for outsourcing and out-tasking requirements, and so forth. <br/><br/>Included processes that manage the Party's lifecycle, such as establishing and terminating a relationship, demographic collection, profiling, and community involvement.<br/>
