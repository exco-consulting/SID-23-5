Collect Party Profile creates a profile for a Party and/or a Party role and collects profile attribute values. For example, a list of hobbies in which a Party is involved. The attribute is placed in a group if it is defined as part of one. Demographics about the Party may become part of the profile if a relationship between the two has been specified.<br/><br/>It can be collected directly from a Party or from another source, such as social media or professional groups to which a Party belongs,.<br/><br/>This process also enables changes to be made to any of these aspects for a profile or a profile's attribute(s).<br/>
