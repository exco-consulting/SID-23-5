Prepare Business Partner Problem process prepares and submits a Business Partner problem to another Business Partner. A submission may supply additional information if requested by the Business Partner. A Business Partner problem may be prepared and submitted by an enterprise as a result of a failure of a product to work or to work ineffectively.<br/>
