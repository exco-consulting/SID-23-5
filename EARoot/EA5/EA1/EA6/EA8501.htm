Specify Party Demographic Definition Group defines a group Party demographic definitions or defines a group of groups.  For example for an organization a group may represent a collection of demographics about the organization itself, such as headquarters location, number and location of offices (domestic vs. international, regions/triads);  each have different access requirements.<br/><br/>This process also enables changes  and deletions to be made to any grouping.<br/>
