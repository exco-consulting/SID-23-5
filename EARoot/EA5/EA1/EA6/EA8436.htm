<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Aged Business Partner Debt Portfolio produces aged Business Partner debt portfolio report to assist in managing Business Partner debt and candidates for write-off. It identifies and possibly writes off a bad Business Partner debt. It establishes and manages commercial arrangements with third parties for the recovery of aged Business Partner debt, and/or selling of parts of the debt portfolio to one or more third parties. It also conducts trend analysis on Business Partner debt.<br/>
