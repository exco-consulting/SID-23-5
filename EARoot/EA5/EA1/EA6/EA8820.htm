Identify Business Partner Revenue Share Discrepancy compares information received from another Business Partner with the revenue share determined by an enterprise. Differences are classified as a discrepancy that is to be resolved. For example, an enterprise's calculated revenue share for a product offering may be missing a product that has been acquired that appears on an bill/invoice from another Business Partner.<br/>
