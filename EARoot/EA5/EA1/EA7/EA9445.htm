<<TMF_Process>>
Plan Data Model task is responsible for gathering and analyzing business requirements to enable design a structure for data based on relationship and data properties.<br/><br/>Plan Data Model will identify the appropriate representation to organize and structure data to support the organization and its business functions. It includes defining business goals for data models, identifying relevant master data, evaluating data sources, analyzing metadata, and analyzing data lifecycle and relationships of data.<br/>
