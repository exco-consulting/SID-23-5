<<TMF_Process>>
Define Enterprise Privacy Location and Regulation process establish how the relevant privacy regulations must be followed with regards to employees and/or stakeholders. This will include the geographical regions where employees/stakeholders operate from/to; and the location of the Data processing entities.<br/>Define Enterprise Privacy Location and Regulation process is used to:<br/>Identify locations (regulatory jurisdiction) impacted by employee and/or stakeholder privacy concerns.<br/>Define the locations where entities processing information related to employees and/or stakeholders will be located<br/>Define the employee and/or stakeholder privacy regulations that apply to locations<br/>Define the dominant regulation<br/>Define the highest standards that apply for multiple legislative locations<br/>
