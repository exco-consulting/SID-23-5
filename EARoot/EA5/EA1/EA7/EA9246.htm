<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Information business activity controls and oversee the management of information, information and data models, information/data integration, and access to information and data from its creation to retirement, so as to optimize its utility, minimize legal risks, and inform business decisions.<br/><br/>Manage Information aligns information with business requirements through service levels and management policies for all data types, including master data, reference data, transactional data, metadata, unstructured data, integrated data, and application data exchange. The process involves activities for data acquisition, creation, modeling, storage, maintenance, processing, distribution, disposition, and archival. Manage Information & Data Lifecycle business activity is in charge of defining the enterprise plan and approach to direct and control Information and data following a lifecycle from creation to final disposal.<br/>This process was renamed in 23.5. Old name was Manage Information & Data<br/>
