Manage Performance Reporting business activity provide the planning and reporting needed to achieve business outcomes.<br/><br/>Performance reporting involves planning (including the production of the priorities and strategies overview and the supplemental information) for any legislative review. It includes reporting on the plans, including annual reports.<br/><br/>Performance reporting is designed to help:<br/>- clarify organizational goals and directions;<br/>- communicate the priorities of the organization;<br/>- monitor progress and make continuous improvement;<br/>- support budgeting and resource allocation decisions; and<br/>- provide information to legislators, the public, media and others about the work of the organization.<br/><br/>Manage Performance Reporting helps to promote a “continuous improvement” feedback loop where reports on activities and performance provide important information to allow for the best possible decision-making in the next planning cycle.<br/>
