<<TMF_Process>>
The Stakeholder Relations Management process grouping focuses on managing the enterprise's relationship with stakeholders and outside entities. Stakeholders include shareholders, employee organizations, etc. Outside entities include regulators, local community, unions. Some of the processes within this grouping are Shareholder Relations, External Affairs, Labor Relations, Public Relations.<br/>
