<<TMF_Process>>
Verify Performance Data business activity handles all tasks that make sure or demonstrate that enterprise performance data are true, accurate or justified. <br/>The tasks here include verifying consistency of independent records of information related to all core processes altogether i.e. market, customer, product, service, business partner and resources within. It works with Manage Performance Consolidation, Reconciliation and Close and undertakes synchronization performance data across all core and enterprise activities for consistency.<br/>
