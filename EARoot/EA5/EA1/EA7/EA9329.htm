Manage Integrity business activity is in charge maintaining the highest ethical standards in every aspect of enterprises' business. <br/><br/>Manage Integrity tasks include assessing integrity, responding to integrity assessment, managing ethics, and managing the enterprises'' reputation. This business activity will leverage Risk Management in supporting the enterprise governance objectives. <br/><br/>Manage Integrity will provide ongoing and systematized processes to guide behaviors which assure integrity of people, processes and products in the enterprise, and with partners. Code Of Conduct (COC) is one of the fundamental artefacts managed by this business activity.<br/>
