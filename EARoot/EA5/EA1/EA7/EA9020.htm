The objective of the Ecosystem Assurance  process is to establish an enterprise-wide ecosystem assurance policy framework, and an associated operational capability aimed at assuring continual trust for customers and ecosystem partners through application of “assurance by design” principles, ensuring that new and digital services work from day one and create value. Ecosystem Assurance  process focusses on providing visibility and traceability of end-to-end digital services, across the partner ecosystem value-chain via enabling and assuring a standardized Open API connectivity, Plug & Play assurance models, multi-way anomaly detection  and accurate multi-partner settlement process.<br/>
