<<TMF_Process>>
Send Employee Privacy Profile process manages the application of privacy policy rules when personal identifiable information must be sent to a data processing entity. <br/>It applies the external rules, then transforms the existing employee privacy profile external rules into internal rules, in order the Data Processor Party can import it into its Privacy Management.<br/>
