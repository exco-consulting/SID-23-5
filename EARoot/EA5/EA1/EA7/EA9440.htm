Manage Unstructured Data Lifecycle business activity directs and controls tasks to manage unstructured data/information from the moment it is created and stored, up to the time it is archived or destroyed or considered useful.<br/> <br/>Manage Unstructured Data Lifecycle tasks to maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of unstructured data throughout its lifecycle. This includes tasks for unstructured data creation and acquisition, unstructured data retention and protection, and unstructured data retirement.<br/>
