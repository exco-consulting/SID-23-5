Plan Supply Network business activity is in charge of integrating purchasing, production, distribution, and transportation so that comprehensive tactical planning and sourcing decisions can be simulated and implemented on the basis of a single, global consistent model.<br/>Plan Supply Network business activity uses advanced optimization activities, based on constraints and penalties, to plan product flow along the supply chain in order to facilitate optimal purchasing, production, and distribution decisions; reduced order fulfillment times and inventory levels; and improved customer service.<br/>
