<<TMF_Process>>
Define Enterprise Privacy Management Definition processes manage the definition of the enterprise privacy management that applies to employees and stakeholders (outside of customers and business partners). <br/>This process is used to determine the legal expectations of entities that want to apply and use employee or other stakeholder information (such as jurisdiction, location, regulation, and standards).<br/>
