<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Enterprise Performance Metric business activity is in charge managing the coordination of the stages of enterprise performance metrics, from analysis, communicating, evaluation and reporting.<br/><br/>Manage Enterprise Performance Metric business activity will define and maintain the specification of Performance metrics by providing, say, high-level description, detailed specification (formulas, thresholds, rules, consequences), applicability, status etc.<br/><br/>Manage Metric also include metric measurement operations: execution of on-demand and recurring collection jobs, rule evaluation, and consequences (actions and notifications).<br/>
