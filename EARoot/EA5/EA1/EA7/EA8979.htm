<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Procedure business activity is in charge of establishing, directing and controlling how to accomplish enterprise activities and tasks. <br/><br/>Manage Procedure business activity includes tasks that establish work instructions that describe specific steps to be handled in a process. This includes defining workflows  or activity flows that include, as an example, how to effectively start a process, when to stop a process or escalate etc.<br/>
