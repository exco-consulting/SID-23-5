Select Training Method business activity makes a choice of the method of training to be used in a training program. Select Training Method  is responsible for guiding selection of the most appropriate training method(s). It takes input from the training assessment activity based on requirements identified between needs and gaps, and recommendations made during the Needs Analysis or training design activities.<br/>
