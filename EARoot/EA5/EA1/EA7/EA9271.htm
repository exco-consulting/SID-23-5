Monitor Data Access task is responsible for insight into when data is in use, who/what is accessing it, and what data, which data is accessed, and why it is accessed.<br/> <br/>Monitor Data Access ensures that data is monitored and secured to mitigate risks. It includes observing all business and technical / application-related functions that require data for a smooth and reliable flow of core, supporting and enabling business processes.<br/>
