Define Knowledge Management Lifecycle business activity is responsible for describing all the stages that support knowledge management across the enterprise. Define Knowledge Management Lifecycle defines 'who', 'what', 'when', 'where' and 'how' of activities that support institutionalizing and operationalizing knowledge management. This includes description of expectation for the creation, sharing, auditing, organizing and storage of knowledge in support of the enterprises business goals.<br/>
