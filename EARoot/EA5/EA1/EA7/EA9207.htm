<<TMF_Process>>
Define Enterprise Privacy Regulation process is used to establish strategic and operating rules that will typically accompany authoritative mechanism for monitoring and enforcing compliance. This will cover the need to:<br/>Identify legislative boundaries and their impact on business/operations.<br/>Define locations for processing of information<br/>Define privacy regulations that apply to locations<br/>Define the dominant regulation<br/>Define the highest standards that apply for multiple legislative locations.<br/>etc.<br/>
