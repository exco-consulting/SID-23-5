Analyze Contract business activity is in charge of examining and tracking all information within a contract over its lifecycle and the impact or implications on the enterprises ongoing concerns. <br/><br/>Analyze Contract business activity is effectively used throughout the contract lifecycle  to address the enterprises need to track implications at any point in time (financial or related to the status of the contract), the impact of clauses, and changes in enterprises operating policy.<br/>Analyze Contract business activity include tasks that perform comparative to previous versions of contracts, if that is beneficial to business. Such tasks include, but are not limited to analyzing contract entities like key clauses & terms, termination & renewal schedules, contracts language (reducing ambiguity) etc.<br/>
