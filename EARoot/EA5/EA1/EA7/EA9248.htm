Manage Enterprise Performance Plan business activity is in charge of controlling and directing the enterprise performance blueprint based on all activities that are required to achieve performance goals and objectives. <br/>In Manage Enterprise Performance Plan business activity, reference is made to the overall purpose/mission of the enterprise based on its desired results by taking stock of outside and inside activities, understanding the enterprises' situation, understanding performance goals and the strategic management goals, along with the objectives that help to achieve the goals.<br/>
