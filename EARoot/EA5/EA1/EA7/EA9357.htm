<<TMF_Process>>
Manage Enterprise Privacy Profile process is used to handle the life cycle of all enterprise privacy profiles. This process is the organizations activities with any engaged party.<br/>It helps all entities processing data to conform with the organization's privacy policies and the laws & regulation under which it operates.<br/>It encompasses:<br/>The Enforcement of the enterprise Privacy Profiles<br/>Import/export of the Privacy Profile for personal identifiable information received or sent<br/>Modify a Privacy Profile, through engaged party consent<br/>Meet regulatory and legal requirements (such as the demonstration of compliance, the 'right of erasure' or 'right to be forgotten'. etc.)<br/>
