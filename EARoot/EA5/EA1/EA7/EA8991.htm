Develop Learning Program business activity establish the overall initiative and scheme to deliver a learning experience required to achieve competence.  Develop Learning Program models and develops the overall learning program to provide trainers/assessors with details about the objectives of learning and assessment; the vocational knowledge, skills or competency that learners are supported to achieve according to an overall program; a profile of the learners and their needs; and suggestions for assessment methods.<br/>
