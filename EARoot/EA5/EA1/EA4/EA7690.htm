<<TMF_Process>>
Assess and plan the approach to be undertaken for activation. <br/>Re-used standard activation processes applicable to specific services. <br/>Provide notifications as required if the activation activity requires a planned outage or is likely to initiate false specific service alarm event notifications. <br/>At the successful conclusion of this activity, the status of the specific services will be changed from allocated to activated, which means they are in-use.<br/>
