<<TMF_Process>>
Service Test Strategy develops the strategies of the enterprise for Service Test.<br/>This process is in charge of identifying types of Service Test to be conducted according to different context (i.e. business activities) for types of Services.<br/>The different context for Service Test are:<br/>   - Service Development & Retirement to qualify the capacity to deliver Services before validating a new ServiceSpecification<br/>   - Service Configuration & Activation to test the Service before closing the ServiceOrderItem<br/>   - Service Quality Management to test Service Quality<br/>   - Service Problem Management to test Service functioning<br/>   - Service Test Management to conduct tests not specific to a customer’s product<br/>
