Manage Service Anomaly Assessment Feedback Reporting business activity is in charge of establishing and handling the activities that prepare and report the feedback based of Service Anomaly Assessment activity.<br/>Manage Service Anomaly Assessment Feedback business activity covers tasks that prepare and report feedback of an Anomaly Assessment activity. This business activity is also helpful in supporting the optimization of the Anomaly Detection business activity, such as fine-tuning know-how for Service anomaly detection.<br/>Reports generated from this business activity can be utilized for Service Anomaly Mitigation and can help to select the consequent actions required to mitigate a Service anomaly.<br/>
