<<TMF_Process>>
Treat Service Exception business activity provides the set of actions that support managing a products’ anomalous exception.<br/>This covers actions that may be:<br/>Proactive: Where the treatment of the conditions leading from the exception are controlled;<br/>Reactive: Where the treatment is directly in response to the exception.<br/>Treat Service Exceptions business activity helps to outline a series of actions that can expedite decisions for mitigation.<br/>
