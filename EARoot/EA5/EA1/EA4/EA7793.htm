Manage Service Performance Reporting business activity is responsible for creating, changing, and publishing reports on service performance.<br/><br/>Manage Service Performance Reporting's goal is to communicate information about service performance to relevant stakeholders. This includes creating reports that accurately reflect current performance levels, making changes to these reports as necessary, and publishing these reports in a timely manner.<br/>
