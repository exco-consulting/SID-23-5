<<TMF_Process>>
The Develop Service Partnership Requirements processes identify the requirements for service aspects sourced from other parties, and the service aspects to be delivered internally to the enterprise. The processes determine the extent to which service aspects need to be sourced externally or delivered from internal capabilities, they scan external availability, and make recommendations about the specific external parties who should be used to provide the service aspects.<br/>Note that the processes used to actually source any external parties occur with the Party Development & Management processes.<br/>
