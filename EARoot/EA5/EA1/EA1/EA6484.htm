<<TMF_Process>>
Onboard Channel Partner business activity is responsible for implementing Channel Partner contracts, signing up of Channel Partners to sales & marketing initiatives, and orientation (training, collaterals exchange, etc.) of Channel Partners into the channel partner program. <br/> <br/>Onboard Channel Partner includes capturing all information about Channel Partners to understand and manage the relationship, appropriately match the channel partner to segments and products/services, performing needs assessment to ensure the Channel Partnership relationship is successful, and defining engagement journeys that support Channel engagements.<br/>
