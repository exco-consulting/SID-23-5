<<TMF_Process>>
The purpose of the Manage Sales Accounts processes is to manage the sales accounts assigned to the sales channel and/or sales manager on a day-day basis. These processes are responsible for contacting the customers associated with each sales account on a regular basis appropriate for the type of account, to develop the appropriate relationships and contacts, to prospect for leads, to promote the enterprise’s product offerings, etc.<br/>
