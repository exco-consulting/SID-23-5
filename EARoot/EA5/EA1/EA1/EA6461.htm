Identify Brand Elements business activity is responsible for determining tangible and intangible visual and emotional elements used to represent the brand. Identify Brand Elements include activities that identify brand name, logo, tag line, color scheme, typography, packaging design, and other visual and auditory cues that will be used to create a consistent brand identity.<br/>
