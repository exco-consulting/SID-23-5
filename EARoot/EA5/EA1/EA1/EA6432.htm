Provide Loyalty Program Status produces a variety of loyalty program status reports, including, but not limited to, churn and uptake, currency earned and redeemed, rewards made and redeemed, and breakage. <br/>Breakage is the difference between points issued and points redeemed. It is the biggest driver of profit for a loyalty program.<br/><br/>Some reports, such as churn, uptake,  and breakage, may use data analytics to generate them.<br/>
