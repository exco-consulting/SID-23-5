Manage Brand Strategy business activity controls the long-term plan for the development of a successful brand positioning in order to achieve specific performance goals. <br/> <br/>Manage Brand Strategy includes creating visual, verbal, and emotional elements of the brand, including the unique name and image for the enterprise as a whole, as well as for the products and/or services. Manage Brand Strategy aims to establish a significant and differentiated presence in the market to attract and retain loyal customers.<br/>
