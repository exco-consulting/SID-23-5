Prepare Market Research Information business activity is responsible for readying the collected market research data for further analysis.<br/><br/>Prepare Market Research Information ensures that market research data is accurate, consistent, and in a format that facilitates effective onward analysis. This includes cleaning the data to remove any errors or inconsistencies, coding qualitative data into a format that can be quantitatively analyzed, and transforming and translating the data as needed.<br/>
