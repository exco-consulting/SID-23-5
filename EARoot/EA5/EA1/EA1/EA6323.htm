Develop Sales Prospect initiates contact with a sales prospect to acknowledge their interest via the Customer or Party Interaction Management process. It also analyzes a prospect's needs and identifies one or more potential product offerings for them. Offerings may expand or change any suggested product offerings that have already been provided to them.<br/>
