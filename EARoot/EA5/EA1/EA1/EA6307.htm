Establish Sales Performance Strategy define and operationalize documented plans that successfully fulfill selling the organization's products and/or service to qualified buyers/consumers. Establish Sales Performance Strategy set clear sales goals and objectives, define compensation plans, and provide guidance for execution and accomplishment of sales initiatives. A Sales Performance Strategy will include, for example, key sales growth goals, sales KPIs, buyer personas, sales processes objectives, competitive analysis, product positioning etc.<br/>
