<<TMF_Process>>
Evaluate Sales Prospect tracks the lifecycle of a sales prospect and records/reports the outcome (win or loss) for a prospect. A prospect represents a “pipeline” of potential sales, each of which is expressed in terms of the probability of successful sales closure and an estimate of the total attainable revenue. The evaluation is also based on the interactions with the sales prospect.<br/>
