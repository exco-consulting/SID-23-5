Conduct Loyalty Program Analysis conducts an enterprise Strength/Weakness/Opportunity/Threat (SWOT) analysis. For example a weakness may be the number one complaint from our customer/partner feedback processes and how can a loyalty program turn this into a strength by introducing a loyalty program. Conduct competitive SWOT, and analyzes a competitor's loyalty program by researching its loyalty program.<br/><br/>The analysis includes researching the loyalty program best practices in an enterprise's industry and in other industries.<br/><br/>The results of the analysis are used to define the program's objectives and strategy.<br/>
