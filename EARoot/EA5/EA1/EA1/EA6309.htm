Manage Sales Performance Dispute business activity directs and controls sales compensation-based performance disputes. Manage Sales Performance Dispute enables dispute management lifecycle for performance-based payouts, sales crediting, and sales performance inquiries. It enables a view on all open disputes, analysts and approve or reject dispute requests, or require additional information about dispute requests.<br/>
