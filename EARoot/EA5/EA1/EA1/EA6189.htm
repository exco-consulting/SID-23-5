Brand Management business process directs and controls activities to manage brand strategy, brand identity elements, brand value, brand image/reputation to consumers, brand performance in the market, and brand equity. <br/><br/>Brand Management business process includes creating and maintaining distinctive brand elements that provide familiarity and differentiation that's not replicable. Brand Management ensures the organization, its products and services, and value propositions perceive its vision, mission, and values in a coherent and consistent way to customers and to the market.<br/>
