Prepare Loyalty Account Communication prepares a loyalty program communication to a participant or for internal use by an enterprise. Communications may be a loyalty program account summary, notification of tier upgrade/downgrade, reward notifications, upcoming expiration or expiration of program currency.<br/><br/>Other Loyalty Program Operation processes may provide information via a notification to this process should be contained on the communication.<br/>
