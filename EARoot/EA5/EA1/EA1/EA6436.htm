Manage Loyalty Program Account Currency provides the ability to expire, reinstate, transfer in/out, adjust, a loyalty participant's account currency.  The Provide Loyalty Account Communication process provides a notification of any of these types of updates.<br/><br/>Note that transfer in/out may be subject to an agreement with a loyalty program partner.<br/><br/>These updates are in addition to those made when earning loyalty program currency or redeeming loyalty program rewards.<br/>
