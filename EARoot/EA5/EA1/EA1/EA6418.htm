Leave Loyalty Program begins and completes the process of leaving an enterprise's loyalty program. A loyalty program participant or an enterprise may initiate this process. An enterprise may initiate the process if the Provide Loyalty Program Operation Report has identified abuse of the program by a participant.<br/><br/>Upon leaving a participant's status may be changed to reflect they are no longer a member, or the participant may be removed (deleted) from the program.<br/>
