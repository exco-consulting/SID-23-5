<<TMF_Process>>
Interpret Market Research Information business activity is responsible for analyzing, identifying and interpreting insights from the data collected during the market research. Interpret Market Research Information transforms raw data into meaningful insights that can inform business decisions with the goal of providing a clear understanding of the research findings. This includes tasks that identify patterns and trends in the data, test market hypotheses, and prepare reports that present conclusions of findings.<br/>
