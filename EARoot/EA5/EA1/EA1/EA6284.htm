Define Loyalty Program Requirements conducts an enterprise Strength/Weakness/Opportunity/Threat (SWOT) analysis, conducts competitive SWOT, and analyze a competitor's loyalty program by researching its loyalty program.<br/><br/>It identifies the core objectives and needs of a loyalty program and loyalty program issues/risks.<br/><br/>It also defines the objectives of the loyalty program, determine the benefits to participants, and identify processes with which the program will interact.<br/>
