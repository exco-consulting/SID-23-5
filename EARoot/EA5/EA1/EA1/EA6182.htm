Channel Management business process directs and controls the channel strategy development, channel model, and channel operations, including reports on intermediaries that deliver products or services to end customers. Channel Management includes all routes to market, including direct and indirect channels, marketing and sales channels, advertising and distributor channels, etc. with the aim of achieving the reach of products and services to customers. It includes channel communications, distribution, incentives and ongoing channel performance management.<br/>
